The 2018 nantong cultural creation design competition will be launched soon

Cultural creativity is the most dynamic element of cultural industry. The 2018 nantong cultural and creative design competition, which aims to stimulate the vitality of cultural innovation, will be launched this month, the reporter learned from nantong Tuesday. The competition consists of six major competitions, involving products, architecture, arts and crafts and many other fields of cultural and creative industry.

Nantong cultural and creative design competition has been held twice, attracted more than 2000 people, more than 3500 entries. Wang yiming, deputy head of the publicity department of nantong municipal party committee, said that the purpose of holding the cultural and creative design competition is to guide the cultural and creative design closely to the public life and combine cultural elements with modern science and technology, artistic value and practical function, creative design and market demand, so as to provide the people with all-round, life-oriented design art products.

It is reported that this design competition is divided into six competitions: product design competition, architecture and environment design competition, public welfare communication design competition, arts and crafts design competition, 2018 first nantong university students design work competition, and 2019 'D+ season' nantong second vocational college art design work competition. Some of the competitions focus on the living needs of the people for food, clothing, housing and entertainment, some focus on exploring the connotation of social thoughts, humanistic spirit and moral concepts, and carry out public welfare communication design competition, while some focus on rural revitalization, and launch environmental creative design competition focusing on rural architecture and livable living. The contest will be officially launched at the end of July and the award ceremony will be held in the first half of next year.

The life of the cultural and creative industry lies in transformation and application. The competition will focus on holding excellent works exhibition and actively promote the effective connection between design products and enterprise demands.

Nantong municipal committee, propaganda minister, for example, liu hao said, gen design contest is a platform and the link, and in the wider gathering creative design resources, to improve the products of industry, construction and the environment in the areas of creative design level, to contest the creative design of booster nantong culture industry transformation and upgrading, and form a new growth point of culture industry.
