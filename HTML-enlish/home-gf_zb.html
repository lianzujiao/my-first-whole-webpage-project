Bamboo weaving art

　


Bamboo weaving is an ancient folk art, which originated in the Ming and qing dynasties.Zhang guo-zheng, a native of chongzhou prefecture (now chongzhou city, chengdu) in sichuan province, loves bamboo weaving. On the basis of learning and summarizing the abundant folk bamboo weaving art in chongzhou, zhang guo-zheng made the bamboo strips thinner and the bamboo threads thin, and the utensils more and more exquisite.Little by little, the bamboo filament had no strength, difficult oneself to form, zhang guozheng chose porcelain, lacquer ware to come as bottom tire, let bamboo plait attach on bottom tire.From this bamboo weaving technique from fetalless molding into the stage of tire attachment.The predecessor of porcelain - shaped bamboo was born.At that time, this purely handmade and exquisite artwork was mostly used as tributes for the royal family.With its technical features of "selected materials, special filament, close to the tire, secret head, multicolored silk", it is unique among many folk crafts.The appearance of fine bamboo weaving, especially the appearance of matted bamboo weaving, makes bamboo weaving from simple and practical into a new realm combining practical and ornamental, stepping into the ranks of arts and crafts.Zhang guozheng's innovation has received attention from all walks of life, and the lacquer lunch box, cigarette box, hat barrel and other products made by him were presented as tributes in the palace, as well as products participated in panama exposition and won silver award.Zhang guozheng was also given "five military products" for his tribute.In the reign of emperor guangxu, zhou xiaohuai of sichuan daotai set up shanemeke under the educational administration, and invited zhang guozheng as a teacher to recruit students and teach the skills of weaving bamboo with fetuses.
