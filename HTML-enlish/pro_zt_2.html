Traditional Culture Public Lecture

　


On May 8th, the “Four Four Trainings” traditional cultural publicity lectures hosted by the Guangxi Branch of Beijing Dexing Tianxia Public Welfare Foundation was held at the Shengde Hall of the No. 28 Zhenhua Road, Gaoxin District, Nanning, and more than 100 people. Students are free to participate in the study. The event invited the teacher to explain the "Four Trainings" in an easy-to-understand manner, aiming at spreading and inheriting the excellent Chinese traditional culture and enhancing the influence of the family-style learning activities.

"Where the Four Trainings" is the family training of the Ming Dynasty Yuan Fanfan combined with his own experience and lifelong learning and cultivation, to educate his own children and grandchildren, is a kind of educational book of morality and self-cultivation. (Guangxi News Network reporter Han Dinghong)
