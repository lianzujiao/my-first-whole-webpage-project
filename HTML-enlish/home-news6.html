At a time when the content market is mixed, "good stories" is the king, and "respect content" is the demand. In 2017, an independent, in-depth, new and creative literature prize -- the first "flint literature prize" came into being.

"Discover good stories and pay homage to good content" -- the first "flint literature prize"

The first "flint prize", sponsored by the writers association of tianjin, tianjin Chinese culture communication company, in the hot, such as drama culture media, reading group jointly undertake, aimed at "salute to discover a good story, good content", through to the domestic outstanding literary works, advocates the spirit of innovation, encourage the original creation, improve work spirit value, open the momentum of literature!
