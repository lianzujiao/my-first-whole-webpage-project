The culture of the kunming municipal committee of the People's Republic of China helps the anti-poverty auction activities

The year 2018 is the decisive year for kunming to take the lead in realizing a well-off society in the whole province. The kunming municipal party committee of mingjin gives full play to the advantages of the main sectors and mobilizes active forces to serve the poverty alleviation work. On August 11, on the occasion of the "creative yunnan 2018 cultural industry expo", an anti-poverty auction activity was held. The proceeds from the auction of ethnic handicrafts will be donated to the primary school of dangui village, xiandian county.

Site, square, vice chair of DPP municipal party committee, municipal party committee united front work department, the city production, poverty relief office, xundian county production run, and artists from the love of DPP, non-material cultural heritage, art lovers, love entrepreneurs and caring person involved in the auction, xundian county osmanthus, head of the village elementary school on behalf of more than 100 people participated in the activities, etc.

The China democracy promotion association is one of the eight democratic parties in China, whose members are mainly representatives of the education cultural circle. Over the years, the China democracy promotion association has made full use of the unique advantages of education and culture to promote the development of education. At the same time, it has actively paid attention to the inheritance and protection of excellent traditional culture of the national people and the people, and made Suggestions for the vigorous development of education and culture.

Kunming committee of the China democratic promotion association has been actively organizing social public welfare service activities, including free training of rural teachers, the introduction of traditional culture into schools, the sending of culture into communities to the countryside and other activities, as well as carrying out the charity sale of painting and calligraphy works and folk handicrafts to donate materials for the development of poverty-stricken areas.

At the same time, the kunming municipal party committee of minjin has a unique advantage in education culture and has been committed to promoting the more vigorous development of education culture for decades. For example: to give full play to the resources of minjin members, to carry out free clinic for ophthalmology and charity sale for painting and calligraphy, and to donate money to poor mountainous areas; To hold the activities of immaterial traditional culture into the campus and culture into the community.

This activity is jointly held by kunming committee of China democratic promotion association and kunming mutaman folk handicraft co., LTD. (China Daily yunnan bureau)
