Opera "sister liu"

　


Liu sanjie is a legendary figure who is deeply loved by the people.Her stories, which range from folk tales to dramas and movies, are constantly in vogue and leading the way.The popular movie "liu sanjie" has attracted numerous audiences at home and abroad, and its popular songs have spread far and wide.The film is based on the transplant of guangxi musical drama "liu sanjie", many of which retain the basic music elements of the original drama.It can be said that musical drama led to the film, the film also promoted musical drama.Different performing arts together to create "liu sanjie" the big red.Although the "fate" of "sister liu" has been ups and downs for many years, its artistic charm has been deeply rooted in people's hearts.

National opera "sanjie liu" adapted from the 1959 edition of guangxi CaiDiao drama "sanjie liu", 1960 projected film music story "sanjie liu" and the 1960 edition of guangxi musical "sanjie liu", tells the story of moving folklore about liu sanjie, shows the beautiful kind, honest and brave, and liu sanjie singing talent, extolling the represented by liu sanjie working people diligence and wisdom, shows the national art of the colorful and beautiful charm of guilin landscape.

Musical drama "liu sanjie" has become our national art garden a gorgeous wonderful work.
