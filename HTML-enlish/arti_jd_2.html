Cantonese opera

　


One of the traditional operas in guangdong, originating from the southern opera, is popular among cantonese communities in lingnan area.Since jiajing years of the Ming dynasty, it has appeared in guangdong and guangxi. It is a performing art combining singing and playing, music by musicians, stage costumes and abstract forms.Each profession of cantonese opera has its own unique costumes and costumes. Cantonese opera was included in the first batch of 518 national intangible cultural heritage list published on May 20, 2006.On September 30, 2009, cantonese opera was approved by UNESCO and put on the world intangible cultural heritage list
