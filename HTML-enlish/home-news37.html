China won the "international military competition - 2018" cultural display "best display country award

Russia Allah pinot July 31 (xinhua) (Yang Qingmin Yang Shengzhao) 31 local time, we in the Russian Moscow pinot training exchange center "the house of friendship" cultural activities "international military competition - 2018" cultural display site, the exhibition of China's martial arts show attracted numerous audience, from the Chinese army's 82th army brigade reconnaissance battalion of soldiers is demonstrating chang chuan martial arts routines, their superb skills won the applause and cheering.

In the cultural display, the Chinese exhibition area is always the exhibition area with more audiences and longer time. In a comprehensive evaluation organized by the Russian ministry of defense, the Chinese team was awarded the first place and won the "best display country award".

This cultural exhibition and exchange activity was jointly organized by 23 participating countries including Russia, China and Iran. It mainly displayed the cultural traditions, national conditions and people's conditions and the construction of armed forces of the participating countries.

China's participating teams present China's national culture and development status to the world in the form of dynamic and static, traditional and modern, through models of military equipment, books and pictures, and various handicrafts, and show Chinese soldiers' martial spirit and excellent quality with martial arts performances.

"In this international cultural show and exchange, we have brought more than 5,000 pieces of exhibits, covering a wide range of different styles, in order to let the world know more about China and its military." In charge of organizing the exhibition of Chinese culture, pan shouyong, commander of a synthesis brigade of the 82nd group army, introduced.

Entering the exhibition area of China, models of domestic advanced military equipment, such as j-20, y-20 and 99-tank, as well as models of fuxing, C919 and long March 5, were neatly placed on the two tables at the front. A Chinese female soldier is explaining patiently to the audience through an interpreter: "these equipment are developed by our country, and their performance has reached the world class level... "

On the right and back of the exhibition area, a 60-inch LCD TV was playing Chinese military parade footage, with a swaggering phalanx and brand-new equipment attracting many viewers. At the back, 8 display boards were erected in order, with pictures and texts, to introduce China's social customs and social conditions and economic development in English and Russian.

The Chinese exhibition area set up guzheng music performance, tea art calligraphy and painting performance, as well as the tri-colored tang dynasty, Peking Opera mask, suzhou embroidery stage screen and other folk exhibits on the exhibition stand, all let visitors linger.
