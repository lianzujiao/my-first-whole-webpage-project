    • The miao ethnic group

      Lusheng music dance is an important part of the cultural life of miao and other ethnic minorities.In each village of the miao nationality, there is a central courtyard dam jumping lusheng. On the night of the moonlit season in summer and autumn, lusheng is singing. All the men, women and children in the village gather together and enjoy the happiness after labor.

    • Zhuang tonggu

      As a percussion instrument in ancient China, tonggu has a history of more than 2,700 years.Originally the drum was used as a drinking vessel (i.e. cauldron), but later it became a percussion instrument.Some of the drums of the zhuang nationality are "wider than a foot" and some are "thicker than two".Tonggu is the most representative cultural heritage of the zhuang nationality and is still loved by the zhuang people and tourists.
