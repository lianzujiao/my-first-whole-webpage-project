Tianlin Yaozu Copper Inspiration

　


The copper inspiration is a form of folk dance created by the people of Muzuo Yao. It is a local art that is popular among the Yao people. It has been circulated in Yaoluo Village, Sanyao Village, Yaocheng Township, Yulin County, Tianlin County. It has a history of more than 200 years and is a beautiful mountain flower in the garden of the motherland culture and art.

The wooden handle Yao copper encourages profound ideological and vivid artistic features. Its rich content and inheritance history are rare in the Chinese national literature and art garden, and it has a wide range of research values ​​such as language, literature, aesthetics, music and art. At present, the copper encouragement has been in danger due to historical and other reasons. It is necessary to strive for census, file, research, publication and other means to create a cultural and ecological village by the establishment of the wooden handle Yao copper, and to establish the "Mujiao Yao Folk Literature and Art Vocational Education Class" in Tianlin County. In order to make the wooden handle Yao copper encourage survival, inheritance and development.
