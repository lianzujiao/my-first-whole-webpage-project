Napo Zhuang folk songs

　


Black-shirted Zhuang is a group of Zhuang people with traditional and unique colors. They claim to be “min”, “zhong” and “ao”. They are now about 51,800 people. They are named after the whole black, and they mainly live on the border between China and Vietnam. Napo County, Guangxi. In the long history, Black Shirt Zhuang still preserves the simple and colorful folk songs, and is known as the “living stone of Guangxi national music rich mine” and Zhuang folk songs.

According to different tones, the long-established black-song Zhuangge can be divided into six categories: “Despite Min”, “On”, “ai”, “Spring Cattle”, “Please Xiange” and “Pan Rui”. set.
