Guangxi ethnic theater construction started to build a new platform for china-asean drama exchanges

　


Nanning, May 23 (reporter Lin yanhua) May 23, guangxi ethnic theater project in nanning held a groundbreaking ceremony.

Chen Guolu introduction, the party secretary of theatre in guangxi guangxi national theatre project total construction area of 18800 square meters, containing 1000 to build a new seat in a medium-sized professional theater and intangible exchange experience hall of 800 square meters, exhibition, in addition also has the guangxi drama drama education center, etc., provide people with the relevant local operas intangible training, classroom and drama set local drama non-material cultural heritage protection, inheritance and communication in the integral whole.After the project is completed, it will become a beautiful scenery line along yongjiang river in nanning city. It will build a new platform for the drama communication between China and asean, meet the new demand of cultural communication at home and abroad, and create new ways and channels for cultural communication and promotion at home and abroad.
