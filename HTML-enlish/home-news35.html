The 2018 bama international marathon will be held in bama, guangxi on oct 21


Lanhaizhou, county chief of bama yao autonomous county, guangxi, introduces the 2018 bama international marathon

China news, July 10 (xinhua) -- the 2018 bama international marathon will be held on October 21 in bama county, known as the "home of the world's longevity", according to the mayor of bama county in guangxi. The event will be organized in accordance with the standard of marathon A of the Chinese athletics association, and will strive to become an important carrier for promoting the achievements of Obama's modernization, internationalization and innovative urban construction.

The same day, a press conference for the 2018 Obama international marathon was held in bama county. The event was jointly hosted by China athletics association, sports bureau of guangxi zhuang autonomous region and the people's government of hechi city, and undertaken by the people's government of bama yao autonomous county.

Blue ocean state is introduced, the Obama international marathon to "health, Obama leads the future" as the theme, to create high degree of internationalization, citizens are widely participate in sports event, carry forward the challenge, dare to transcend the marathon spirit, further promote the the development of the national fitness campaign, the efforts to improve public well-being.

According to the introduction, the competition will start at 8 o 'clock on October 21. The main venue of the event is located in the sports square east of bama city. The track mainly centers around shouxiang avenue, chengbei road, minzu road, people's safety road, and the road leading into the city. In this way, athletes can experience the beautiful scenery, landscape features and humanistic features of bama in sports, and fully present the city culture and features of bama. The competition consists of three events: men's and women's full marathon (42.195km), men's and women's half marathon (21.0975km) and joy run, among which the full marathon is limited to 3000 participants, half marathon is limited to 3000 participants and joy run to 4000 participants, with a total scale of 10000 participants.

　The event will be conducted in accordance with the latest track and field competition rules approved by the China athletics association and the competition rules of this marathon. The competition awards are generous, including the marathon, half-marathon ranking award and the Obama prize. There are no prizes for happy running, and the total prize is RMB 138,200. Among them, the top eight winners were selected for the full marathon ranking, and the top eight winners were awarded with RMB 20,000, 15,000, 10,000 to 1,000 yuan. Half marathon rank prize male, female distribution admission top 8. The race is specially set with the Obama runner's award. All the players who participate in the marathon and half marathon, and achieve the top 20 results of male and female, will be awarded with the award of 300 yuan to 1000 yuan respectively, which can be accumulated.
