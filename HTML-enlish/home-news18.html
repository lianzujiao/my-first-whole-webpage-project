2018-01-31 Source: China culture daily 　

Interpretation of national public culture cloud live public library law

Recently, the ministry of culture national public culture development center (hereinafter referred to as the "development center") and culture sharing project in hunan province branch, jointly organized by the network training, invited national public culture service system construction, a professor at Peking University, director of the expert committee Anton on "public library law interpretation" courses, all over the country more than 130000 grassroots culture through the backbone is a test run of the live national public cultural cloud - sharing together to learn. This is the first training session held by the development center this year.

　This live broadcast training is specially targeted at all branches of the cultural sharing project and part-time staff of the basic comprehensive cultural service center. The training makes a deep and simple explanation on the legislative background, legislative process, main contents of legal provisions and the significance and far-reaching influence of this law on the development of public library. A total of 115,000 people watched the broadcast through the "national public culture cloud" mobile APP and WeChat client, accounting for 87.8% of the total viewing, highlighting the convenience of mobile service. In addition, local grassroots cultural backbone can continue to learn on the "national public cultural cloud" by means of on-demand broadcasting.

Network training is an important means for the development center to implement the construction of grassroots cultural backbone team. In 2017, the development center, combined with the pilot work of "national public cultural cloud", intensified efforts to carry out network training through mobile terminals, with the total number of training targeted at grassroots level exceeding 2.04 million, an increase of 56% over 2016. This year, moocs will be combined with research and development to further enhance the systematic, targeted and interactive nature of online training.
