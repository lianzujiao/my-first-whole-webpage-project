In the past 10 years, the actors of guangxi 70 - day osmanthus opera have drawn 48 pieces of face to inherit intangible cultural heritage

　


There are two peaches on the forehead of the mask is zhang fei, there is a tiger claw is li kui, there is a fire gourd is mengliang...These lifelike GUI drama facebook is the guangxi liuzhou GUI drama old actor zhou xiaohe works.A few days ago, the reporter approached zhou xiaohe, to find out he and guiju drama "can not love."

Zhou xiaohe comes from a family of pear garden, is a national first-class actor, born in 1943, has been over 70 years now, from 1954 into the liuzhou GUI opera troupe, he has been connected with GUI opera for more than 60 years.In 2005, zhou xiaohe began to paint the character face of guiju opera after he retired as deputy director of liuzhou GUI opera troupe.

For nearly a decade, zhou xiaohe has been actively promoting the inheritance of traditional culture of guiju opera, and he has been making efforts to call attention to this intangible cultural heritage by drawing the mask of guiju opera

Guangxi opera is a kind of local opera opera in guangxi. In recent years, due to the lack of venues, brain drain, capital shortage and other problems, guangxi opera is on the verge of decline and suffers inheritance crisis.Approved by the state council in 2006, guiju opera was included in the first batch of national intangible cultural heritage list.
