"Red angel" miss tourism culture world (huaihua division) press conference held


（The 2018 "red angel" miss tourism culture competition (huaihua division) press conference is held at huaihua hongya hospital on August 5.

Huaihua news network (correspondent liang chao) on August 5, 2018 "red angel" miss tourism and culture world (huaihua competition area) press conference held in huaihua hongya hospital.

This contest is the annual cultural event authorized by miss tourism and culture of the world, dragon king international media, hosted by huaihua city radio and television station, named by the general head of huaihua hongya hospital, and co-aired by huaihua city radio and television station and mango ray video. Guided by the national strategy of building "beautiful China, beautiful countryside" and "One Belt And One Road" proposed by the 19th CPC national congress, it aims to select the most outstanding talents for cultural tourism promotion, recommend and disseminate the profound folk culture and advantaged cultural tourism resources. Drive the comprehensive influence of the city by cultural events, and enhance the brand image of huaihua tourism culture.

It is reported that this competition (huaihua competition area) will use the new (Internet + new media + live broadcasting) mode, integrate the characteristic tourism and cultural elements of huaihua city, and create "beauty + culture + health + confidence" huaihua tourism and cultural promotion ambassador. The competition consists of news conference, audition, rematch, training, final and award show. The champion, Asian champion and third runner-up will represent huaihua in the national finals in mid-october 2018. At that time, tencent, netease, KK live TV and live platforms will broadcast the national finals around the world simultaneously. New media such as mango TV, mango rui, WeChat, diaofeng, panda TV and douyu will give full support to jointly build a strong platform for audience rating communication and spread huaihua's unique scenery and profound culture to the world.

At the press conference, the organizing committee gave a detailed explanation of the event background, brand positioning, competition system, event implementation and the overall situation of previous events.
