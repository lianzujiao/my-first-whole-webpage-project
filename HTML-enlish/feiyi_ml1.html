Buluotuo

　


Bloch tuo is a mythical figure zhuang clan tribal society, may be a clan tribal leaders, as a result of his historical contribution and enjoys a high status in this clan, tribe, people add their native culture to create on him, his apotheosis, make him a ancestors of zhuang and its homologous ethnic identity of creation god and ancestor god and moral.On the basis of folk myths, stories and legends, the ancestors of the zhuang nationality who love to sing, took the most common five-character pai songs in the zhuang folk songs as the basic form to sing the magnificent achievements of the first ancestor buluotuo, who opened the sky and created human beings, and formed an epic of the oral biography of the zhuang nationality.In the tang and song dynasties, the zhuang people appeared the ancient zhuang characters (also known as vernacular characters) with Chinese characters, sounds and meanings.From about the Ming dynasty, this oral epic could be preserved in the form of ancient zhuang characters while being sung orally.

The prologue epic of zhuang brotuo has profound connotation, unique style and its own system.It mainly consists of the following six aspects: buluotuo created heaven and earth, man, everything, earth emperor, writing almanac and ethics.In addition, the folk sacrifice of buluotuo has the content of singing buluotuo, and the buluotuo song wei also has about singing the song of buluotuo.Before the song wei starts, people must sing the ancient songs of buluotuo, including sacrifice songs and creation songs. In the long process of inheritance, the epic passed down from the mouth of the zhuang buluotuo has formed the basic features of universality, original content, original nature and superb artistry.
