Liu sanjie culture: "poetry and distance" in guangxi

2017-03-29　Source: guangming.com　

Kwangmyong March 29 - (Wu Xiuhuan Zhou Shixing) liu sanjie culture brand seminar held 28 in guangxi university for nationalities, of the 15 from colleges and universities, publishing, research institutions, experts and scholars gathered back "zhuang SAN yue SAN" cultural roots - liu sanjie culture, and discusses how to from the cultural needs of modern people to liu sanjie culture inheritance and development, make recommendations for the development of liu sanjie culture.

Professor huang xiaojuan, vice President of guangxi nationalities university, said that liu sanjie's cultural brand is rich in connotation, and it is not only from guangxi, but also from China and the world. This symposium will promote the heritage and protection of liu sanjie's culture, which is conducive to enhancing the cultural soft power of guangxi.


Vice principal huang xiaojuan's speech

Cao guangzhe editor-in-chief address

Cao guangzhe, vice-chairman and editor-in-chief of guangxi publishing media group co., LTD., believes that liu sanjie culture is the iconic composition of the "zhuang people's march and march". Liu sanjie is the daughter of song wei, and the festival activities of the zhuang nationality can't be separated from the traditional singing customs. This symposium discusses the historical origin, essence, cultural value, practical significance and inheritance and development of liu sanjie's culture from an academic point of view, which is a gift to the "zhuang people's march and march" and is of great significance for promoting the cultural construction of guangxi.

It is understood that from the 1960 "phenomenon level" film "liu sanjie" in the empty lane, to the 2004 "impression · liu sanjie" in the mountains and waters created by zhang yimou and others, to the performance of the 2017 Spring Festival gala in guilin For decades, the singing of "liu sanjie" has been lingering and continuous.

Among liu sanjie culture, ballad culture is the most popular. Ballad culture is created by the people of all ethnic groups in guangxi in labor production and daily life, and is the crystallization of the wisdom of all ethnic groups. As a country, a nation and a region in the entire culture of the most permanent charm of the wonderful accumulation and spirit endowment is the most important part of regional soft power. Guangxi is lucky to have such a well-known cultural brand as liu sanjie culture, we need to consciously inherit and protect.
