80 measures to promote economic and cultural exchanges and cooperation between Guangxi and Taiwan

2018-08-02 22:11 source:Guangxi News Network 　


Guangxi News Network Nanning August 2 (Reporter Huang Xinyue Huang Lingna) On the 2nd, the reporter learned from the press conference on "Some Measures to Promote Economic and Cultural Exchanges and Cooperation between Guangxi and Taiwan" that the Taiwan Affairs Office of the People's Government of the Autonomous Region and the Development and Reform Commission of the Autonomous Region Issued and implemented "Several Measures on Promoting Economic and Cultural Exchanges and Cooperation between Guangxi and Taiwan" (hereinafter referred to as "several measures").

Li Dongxing, director of the Taiwan Affairs Office of the autonomous region, made a press release on "Several Measures", introduced the significance, main contents, characteristic highlights and implementation of the "Several Measures", and attracted the highlights of Taiwan-funded enterprises in "Several Measures" and introduced Taiwan. The experience of advanced agricultural production and management helped the accurate poverty alleviation, and the promotion and operation of the industrial cooperation zones on both sides of the Guangxi Strait answered questions from reporters. Yan Bogui, deputy director of the Autonomous Region Development and Reform Commission, answered questions from reporters on the issue of Taiwanese youth entrepreneurship and employment, Taiwan talent introduction measures, and promotion of Taiwan-funded enterprises enjoying the same treatment as Guangxi enterprises. The meeting was hosted by Li Wen, deputy director of the Taiwan Affairs Office of the autonomous region.

The reporter learned that there are a total of 80 measures, including promoting investment and economic cooperation, promoting learning and entrepreneurship, facilitating living, deepening social and cultural exchanges and cooperation, and promoting the construction of cooperation zones, and put forward specific measures to promote economic and cultural exchanges and cooperation between Guangxi and Taiwan. To provide Taiwanese compatriots with the same treatment as Guangxi residents in investment, entrepreneurship, employment, study and life in Guangxi. This measure has four highlights highlighting the advantages of Guangxi, highlighting policy innovation, attaching importance to youth and poverty alleviation, and synchronizing preferential policies with the cooperation zone.
