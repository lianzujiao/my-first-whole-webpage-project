National costume culture

　



Jin, one of the four famous Chinese brocades, originated from the Han Dynasty, is a gem of Guangxi national culture. Zhuang Brocade is a national intangible cultural heritage with a long history and profound cultural heritage.

Zhuang Brocade, together with Yunjin, Shujin and Songjin, is also known as China's four famous brocades. It is said to have originated in the Song Dynasty and is a cultural treasure of the Chinese nation. Zhuang Jin Zhuang is called mbaw laiz fax, which means the page of the sky, which is made of cotton or silk. The vivid pattern, rigorous structure, colorful, full of warm and cheerful national style, reflects the Zhuang people's worship of the heavens and the earth and the pursuit of a better life. Luancheng County is one of the origins of Guangxi Zhuangjin. It has a long history and profound cultural heritage. Lucheng Brocade was once a fine product of Guangxi Zhuangjin, and it was a tribute to the Royal Palace.

Common brocade machines include bamboo cage brocade machines, Longzhou brocade machines and Jingxi brocade machines, all of which have their own frame structure and weaving principles. They are popular in the local area and conform to local brocade habits.

After more than a thousand years of development, Zhuangjin has three kinds of patterns of self-contained system, one is a continuous pattern mainly composed of geometric decorative patterns, the other is a composite pattern with flowers as the theme, and the third is a pattern with the theme of auspicious beasts. . Traditionally used patterns include 蟒 dragon pattern, marigold pattern, Futian pattern, 4D daisy pattern, pattern, water pattern, moiré, flower, animal, etc. More than 20 kinds of patterns, vivid patterns, rigorous structure, colorful, full of warm and cheerful National style.
