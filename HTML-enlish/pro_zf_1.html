Press conference of "Zhuangzu March 3·Bagui Carnival" was held

　



"The Zhuang Nationality March 3 · Bagui Carnival" is one of the important activities of the 60th anniversary of the celebration of the autonomous region. It aims to fully demonstrate the practical achievements of Xi Jinping's new era of socialism with Chinese characteristics in the land of Bagui. Demonstrate Guangxi as the country's most populous ethnic minority autonomous region's colorful national traditional culture, showcasing the unity, harmony and stability of the cadres and the masses in the region

It is understood that this year is the 60th anniversary of the establishment of the autonomous region, and it is also the fifth year for Guangxi to determine the "March 3rd of Zhuang" as a statutory holiday. It is the work of the party committee and government of the autonomous region to implement the cultural tourism consumer brand of "Zhuang Nationality March 3·Gui Jia Jia Nian". the second year. To build a cultural tourism consumer brand of "Zhuang Nationality March 3 · Bagui Carnival" is an important measure for Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region to implement the internship in the new era of socialism with Chinese characteristics and the spirit of the 19th Party Congress. It is important to celebrate the 60th anniversary of the establishment of the autonomous region. One of the activities aims to fully demonstrate the practical achievements of Xi Jinping's new era of socialism with Chinese characteristics in the land of Bagui, show the unique superiority of the party's national policy and the system of regional ethnic autonomy, and show Guangxi's economic and social development as a frontier ethnic minority region. Brilliant achievements and bright prospects show Guangxi as the country's most populous ethnic minority autonomous region's colorful national traditional culture, show the cadres and the masses in the region united as one, a harmonious and stable life, and further stimulate the majority of party members and cadres and people of all regions. Strive to advance the construction of the "three major ecological", Accelerate the realization of "two completions", solidly promote the prosperity of the people, and strive to write a new chapter in the development of Guangxi in the new era.
