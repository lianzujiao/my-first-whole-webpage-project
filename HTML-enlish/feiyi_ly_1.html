Sanjiang dong autonomous county, colorful intangible heritage introduced guests

　


In recent years, sanjiang dong autonomous county, relying on the colorful and unique advantages of intangible cultural heritage such as building Bridges, spinning yarn, baijia banquet and singing and dancing, has set out a precise path of poverty alleviation and poverty alleviation through the mode of "poverty alleviation + tourism".From January to August 2017, the county received 6102,000 tourists.

"intangible cultural heritage" is an important feature of living inheritance. It mainly relies on the oral and oral teaching of the inheritor.
