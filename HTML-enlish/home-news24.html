Guizhou "post-90s" students learn from teachers to learn arts intangible cultural heritage from generation to generation

　On July 12, in the "social education and non-genetic inheritance dialogue activity" hosted by guizhou provincial department of culture, 15 "post-98" students, including Yang mei and long zaiwang of guizhou shenghua vocational college, were honored as their teachers by Yang xifen, Yang min, a batik master, and zhang qing, a folk embroidery master, in the presence of guests.

"Batik is a traditional folk textile printing and dyeing handicraft of China's ancient ethnic minorities. I want to say to my disciples, thank you for choosing this path." Yang xianfen, a batik craftsman, said she will spare no effort to play the role of "transmission, help and belt" and teach students her craftsmanship without reservation, hoping that the intangible cultural heritage of guizhou can be inherited.

Under the influence of her family at home, Yang mei, who lives in a nice village in huaishui town, guizhou, is very enthusiastic about the intangible cultural heritage of guizhou. After three years of study in the middle school of tang renfang intangible cultural heritage college of shenhua vocational college, guizhou, she thinks that she still has poor skills, so she continues to choose higher vocational education. At the ceremony, Yang mei expressed her determination to inherit the intangible cultural heritage to her master Yang xifen. "since childhood, she loved painting and batik, and was very willing to learn this skill. She would inherit and carry forward the intangible cultural heritage of guizhou minorities with a serious and diligent attitude."

Today is of great significance to zhang qing, who has been an embroidery teacher at shenhua vocational college in guizhou for nearly 4 years. This is the second batch of "disciples" that zhang qing, a folk embroidery craftsman, received after coming to shenhua college.

"Embroideries are also very lucky and bitter. Embroidery can hurt the eyes and spine to a certain extent, but they are not afraid of pain and are willing to learn." Talking about the future teaching of "apprentices", zhang said that when she saw them sitting quietly in class, she seemed to see a number of excellent inheritors of the art of embroidery in the future.

In the worship ceremony, the apprentice presented the teacher with the attachment, the gift of the teacher, the grand ceremony of kneeling and the tea, and the master presented the apprentice with the certificate. The apprentice made requirements and expectations, and the process was full of solemn and solemn ceremony feeling.

Guizhou shenghua vocational college in 2012 and Beijing tang fang company in-depth cooperation between colleges and intangible master apprentice, make and manage the apprentice, a young girl's bedroom namely workshop, dyeing is the classroom, teachers and students joint design and product, realize engineering one of the real integration of teaching management, among them, the student collective production work repeatedly referred to as "national gift". Teaching of integrated rural girl familiar with embroidery, batik, dances, songs, such as national culture elements, design principle of real integration embroidery, batik, dancing, activity curriculum, take "work is work, work is a commodity product, the product is" the transformation of "the doctrine of" experience teaching, make students realize the study or work, internships or employment culture education.

It is learned that the guizhou shenhua vocational college has trained 265 rural girls since its founding, and the employment rate of girls after graduation is 100%. (reporter hong yingjie, huiwei zhang)
