Liu sanjie ballad

　


"Now guangxi into the sea, are three sisters mouth spread."The zhuang people believe that xing ge wei is the song spread by liu sanjie, or to commemorate liu sanjie's "fairy transformation" and sing it regularly.Liu sanjie is an important symbol of the universal formation of ge wei.People believe that the songs of song wei are those of liu sanjie.Yizhou is a typical area where liu sanjie's ballads are abundant.In the rich and deep soil of folk songs and country songs, there appeared the singing fairy liu sanjie and formed the colorful singing culture of liu sanjie. Yizhou is also recognized as the hometown of liu sanjie.

The ballad of sister liu is divided into seven categories: life song, production song, love song, ceremony song, riddle song, story song and creation ancient song.The ballad of liu sanjie has such characteristics as the excellence of poetic mode of thinking, the distinct national character of taking root in the cultural tradition of the community, the integrity of inheritance sequence, the diversity of ballad types and the richness of cultural connotation, the pervasiveness of communication and the far-reaching influence.Liu sanjie's ballad has exerted a profound influence on the whole country and even the whole world, showing the artistic charm of the living culture of the Chinese nation. It not only has the cultural history value of witnessing the national history and emotional expression mode, but also has the research value of ethnology, anthropology, sociology, aesthetics and other disciplines.However, with the traditional social foundation of zhuang nationality of "relying on songs to match" no longer exists, the impact of strong culture and new forms of entertainment leads to the lack of people to follow the ballad of liu sanjie, and the old ballad of liu sanjie is in danger of being lost, so it must be protected.
