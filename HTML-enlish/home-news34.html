"New era civilization teaching center" was unveiled in hengxian county, guangxi

　Addition to nanning on August 1 (Zhong Jianshan) in guangxi zhuang autonomous region sports bureau deputy director lu 1 Italian in nanning, said on August 8 to 15, guangxi nanning will host the 2018 China go conference (nanning) in guangxi, China - asean international chess tournament, the 2018 season city go 1/8 league final game regular season and playoffs finals series of major chess tournament. At that time, the well-known go, chess, bridge players from home and abroad will gather nanning to compete; At the same time, artificial intelligence go AI such as jiaoyi, xingfen go and super lira will also play a different "game" in this area.

　In recent years, lui said, guangxi has put forward the goal of "turning the sport around and rejuvenating the sport of guangxi" in an effort to build an important regional competition center for asean. After years of development, guangxi chess and card movement has made remarkable progress in terms of competitive level and industrial development. China-asean chess and card international invitational tournament has become an important platform for communication between China and asean. The year 2018 is the 60th anniversary of guangxi zhuang autonomous region. Guangxi sports bureau will make efforts to build guangxi into a Chinese intellectual sports industry base by taking such major events as the 2018 China go congress and the 2018 china-asean chess and card international invitational tournament as the carrier, and promote the development of intellectual sports in asean countries.

　According to the introduction, the 2018 China go congress is hosted by the China go association, the sports bureau of guangxi zhuang autonomous region, the people's government of nanning, and undertaken by nanning sports bureau, nanning sports federation and huachi weilian sports industry corporation. Conference held on August 8 to 15, will build to a formal event route, supplemented, synchronized events events include: 2018 "citic securities cup" smart world go open, Chinese man go league race wins, China's women go special conference, the national league China go go set engineering national tournament game to game, millions and millions of engineering universal on go go stage a chess tournament, the national team tournament (children), China's rural go league finals of 2018 and 2018, get together Chinese professional players, chess and so on more than 40 events and activities.

According to tan yonggang, general manager of weilian sports industry co., LTD., the 2018 "citic securities cup" world intelligent go open will be held simultaneously during the China weiqi conference, and the event will be divided into the original group and the open group. The AI teams of artificial intelligence, such as jiaoyi, xingzhen go, lira, Raynz, og-go and ElfOpenGO, will have interesting duels. In addition, the session will also be a "artificial intelligence + go industry application exhibition" (and 2018 Chinese go meeting expo), heaven and earth, stones, guest star array, implement, qizhi, sheng chess, new Po, love chess, chess, hikaru no go, hidden wisdom, love, stone and so on more than a dozen Chinese and foreign well-known enterprise to the artificial intelligence, AI technical results of practical application in the field of weiqi.

　It is worth mentioning that during the competition, famous players from the Chinese and foreign go circles, such as nie weiping's 9th Dan, ma xiaochun's 9th Dan, ke jie's 9th Dan, guli's 9th Dan, park yanhuan's 9th Dan and liu changhe, the general manager of the Korean chess institute, will attend the event. The fun activities of integrating go competition and entertainment will also be held simultaneously.

　To expand the scale and influence of the event, tang hong, director of the nanning sports management training center, said that the "BDF totem building materials cup" 2018 China - asean chess and card international invitational will be held at the same time with the China go congress. The competition includes the 14th china-asean weiqi international invitational, the 13th china-asean bridge international invitational and the 10th china-asean chess international invitational. Among them, 18 teams from 15 countries and regions including China, 9 asean countries (myanmar), the United States and Canada were invited to the 14th china-asean go international invitational.
