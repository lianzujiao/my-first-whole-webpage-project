Guigang City’s 2018 “Drama Walks into the Campus” Tour Event Enters Daling Junior High School

　

People's Daily Online, Sanjiang, June 13th On June 13, in Sanjiang Middle School, Sanjiang Dong Autonomous County, Guangxi, students were learning to play the Yi people's songs. In recent years, the county has vigorously promoted the national culture into the campus activities. Through the methods of grasping teachers, catching dolls, grasping classes, and grasping activities, the county has introduced “non-legacy” cultures such as Yangge, Qiang embroidery, enamel paintings and Yi instrumental music into the classroom. While enriching the cultural life of the school students in the township, it is also a powerful way to promote the "non-legacy" skills of the Yi people from generation to generation. (Pang Geping, Gong Pukang)
