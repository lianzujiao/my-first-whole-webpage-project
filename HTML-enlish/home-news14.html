Interpretation of methods and series of benchmarks (trial)

The guangxi zhuang autonomous region cultural administrative penalty discretion applies to (try out) "and" the guangxi zhuang autonomous region cultural non-material cultural heritage administrative penalty discretion benchmark (try out) "the guangxi zhuang autonomous region, the social cultural art, employs administrative penalty discretion benchmark (try out)" the guangxi zhuang autonomous region cultural cultural market administrative penalty discretion benchmark (try out) "the guangxi zhuang autonomous region, cultural relics administrative penalty discretion benchmark (try out)" and so on 10 benchmark (hereinafter referred to as "method and a series of benchmark (try out)") has been autonomous regions cultural department of the party group meeting examined and approved, Effective nov 25, 2017. "Method and a series of standards (try out)" will make clear further standardize the cultural administrative penalty discretion, to ensure that the culture of administrative punishment, fairness and openness "method and a series of standards (try out)" from illegal discretion class, basic rules of discretion and implement discretion benchmark system requirements of constructing cultural administrative penalty discretion benchmark.

I. background for the establishment of the method and series of benchmarks (trial)

Series of the measures and the benchmark (try out) "is based on the general office of the people's government of the guangxi zhuang autonomous region, on the implementation of the comprehensive build system of administrative discretion benchmark opinions (cassia ZhengBan hair [2017] no. 49) spirit, the implementation of the autonomous region comprehensively promote administration according to law work leading group about 2017 OuZhi the realistic need, which the authority for examination and assessment of the rule of law government construction work (guangxi administration according to law do hair [2017] no. 3), as well as strengthen the administrative law enforcement in our culture, standardize the cultural administrative penalty discretion, to ensure that the culture, the important measures for administrative punishment of fair public It is of great practical significance for the establishment and improvement of the cultural administrative discretion standard system, the refinement and quantification of the cultural administrative discretion standard, and the unification of the range, type and range of the discretion.

i. Necessity of the establishment of the method and series of benchmarks (trial)

One is according to the general office of the people's government of the guangxi zhuang autonomous region on the implementation of the comprehensive build system of administrative discretion benchmark opinions (cassia ZhengBan hair [2017] no. 49) deployment requirements, by the end of 2017, the autonomous region's administrative organs according to law to establish and improve the system of administrative discretion benchmark system, refinement, quantitative administrative discretion standard, unified specification this system the scope of administrative discretion, the type and magnitude.

ii. The second is the need to further standardize the standard of cultural law enforcement discretion. Administration according to law requires authorities at all levels to exercise administrative power strictly in accordance with legal limits of authority and procedures, standardize administrative law enforcement, and improve their administrative capacity in accordance with the law. In order to avoid the different penalties imposed by cultural law enforcement departments at all levels in different regions and in different periods, it leads to different punishments for the same case. Therefore, the establishment of the discretion base system can better regulate cultural law enforcement behavior, limit the discretionary space, prevent the arbitrariness of administrative law enforcement, and improve the predictability and transparency of administrative penalty behavior, which is conducive to protecting the legitimate rights and interests of administrative counterparts and improving the efficiency of administrative penalty work.

iii. The feasibility of formulating the method and series of benchmarks (trial)

First, develop the methods and series of standards (try out) "is on the basis of years of cultural development of our law enforcement practice, give full consideration to the differences between the district economy development and the law enforcement environment, with reference to also consider the district economic development level and the difference between developed areas, to further refine legal punishment standard, ladder; Secondly, the general office of the people's government of the guangxi zhuang autonomous region, on the implementation of the comprehensive build system of administrative discretion benchmark opinions (cassia ZhengBan hair [2017] no. 49) specified in the autonomous region's administrative organs according to law to establish and improve the system of administrative discretion benchmark system, refinement, quantitative administrative discretion standard, unified specification this system the scope of administrative discretion, the type and scope; Thirdly, the formulation of "methods and series of benchmarks (trial)" reflects the self-restraint and self-supervision of cultural law enforcement agencies, and improves the efficiency of law enforcement while realizing the self-supervision of executive power.
