Nanguo book festival activities rich interesting, readers enjoy the spiritual culture feast

The 2018 southern book festival and yangcheng book fair kicked off at pazhou exhibition center in guangzhou on the morning of October 10.

Despite a light rain in the morning, pazhou exhibition center is not less popular. In the afternoon, with a number of celebrity activities held, the number of people on the scene has reached a small peak. This year's southern book festival is the third year that the organizing committee advocates "smart book fair". With the development and application of official APP of the southern book festival and the application of various artificial intelligence means, the "scientific and technological content" of the southern book festival is higher and higher, and the pace of people visiting the book fair is more and more relaxed.

　A variety of new and interesting activities for the book festival in southern China added a lot of color. A total of 56 cultural activities were held on the site of nanguo book festival in just 10 days, including reader sharing sessions, lectures, book signings, new book launches, interactive games and other forms. Readers of all ages can find their own cultural activities at the festival.

Novel experience▶▷New Zealand pavilion performs Maori traditional dance

There were a lot of activities related to teenagers on the first day of the opening ceremony. In the interactive area of shaanxi xinhua publishing media group in the exhibition hall on November 2, the staff patiently instructed the children to learn how to rubbber the four god wattan: first wet the paper with spray, then cover the paper with towel and slowly rub the paper to wattan; Wait for paper a little dry, and then use black printing clay carefully rubbing more than 40 minutes before the final product. Despite the low "pass rate" of finished products, children are still in high spirits. At the scene of the kookaburra at the children's intelligence, the staff patiently guide children with daily life simple material hand made a "dog" : wire as the axis, all sorts of color in the orderly winding around the "stars", from "the dog" body torso to tail, forming step by step, finally presents a lifelike "dog".

The New Zealand pavilion, as a new pavilion, is also a novel experience for readers. At 11pm, the New Zealand pavilion staged a Maori traditional haka dance, with dancers flapping and Shouting to show the pre-war morale boost. In contrast to the reading promotion area, the ancient shadow puppetry art was brought to the scene of the book festival, and the innovative shadow puppetry cat miro aroused children's interest in traditional art. At the end of the performance, the children were eager to step forward and learn how to play shadow puppetry. In the book festival, not only the novel culture can be accepted by readers, but also the traditional cultural form can glow new glory.

"Spiritual feast"▶▷Reader sharing focuses on youth growth

Veteran Wen Yishe huacheng publishing house, this time also for young children to offer two rich feast "spirit" - the famous children's literature theorists, children's book publisher Sun Jianjiang and picture book experts Sun Yuhu held "to the flower city" original children's literature of ling book launch, and guangdong and experimental middle school language teacher ding in guangzhou, a professor at the university, famous writer Luo Hong around "reading, awaken the power of growth" of the conversation. These two activities about the growth of teenagers attracted a large number of readers and parents stop to appreciate.

　At the "coming to little flower town" campaign, several young children's literature writers Shared their stories with readers. Meng fei, winner of the special prize of "zhouzhuang cup" national short story competition for children's literature, bing xin's new works award for children's literature, recalled his childhood in "old garden of flutes". Guo jiangyan, winner of Chen bo's international children's literature prize and winner of the central propaganda department's "five projects" award, wrote the joy and sadness of childhood in the simple style of "shaking ah yan", which can be called the Chinese village version of "little peas by the window"

　"I think learning Chinese is a very happy thing. Reading can bring so much to me." "Reading, awakening the power of growth," a junior student told reporters after the sharing session. As a famous Chinese teacher in guangdong experimental middle school, ding zhijing and luo hong not only Shared their experiences in teaching and reading, but also gave some Suggestions on how to select Chinese books for different students.

　In the literature salon area of the pavilion, famous writer wu meizhen Shared her experience and experience in writing for many years. In this "book fragrance -- family reading theme activity", students and teachers performed a wonderful program combining allegro and classic recitation, and the audience of parents were enthusiastic.

Wisdom book fair▶▷Booking electronic tickets is free of queuing annoyance

Book festival scene, cultural products also attracted many readers attention. The reporter learned in the on-the-spot interview that the students came to the book fair, in addition to buying books and participating in cultural activities, the exquisite cultural products also let them linger. Hand tent tape, three-dimensional greeting CARDS, etc. also attracted a large number of visitors.
