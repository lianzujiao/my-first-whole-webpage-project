Yao Family Bird Festival

　


The Yao people live in the mountains (there is a saying that "there is no mountain and no Yao"). The mountains in southern China are full of trees and birds, so the Yao people are closely related to the birds and have deep feelings. Therefore, there are more customs and legends related to birds in Yaoshan. On the first day of the second lunar month, the busy spring ploughing has not yet begun. On this day, the Jianghua Yao people wore their national festival costumes and happily carried out activities such as feeding birds, birds and songbirds, and celebrating the Birds Day. On the one hand, they wish the birds to be happy and prosperous; on the other hand, they also learn the free life of birds. In the early morning, the Yao dynasty was as cheerful as the Han Chinese New Year. The adults and children were all busy. They inserted their own round plaques in the squares of the houses and the four corners of the fields. They kept silently chanting the traditional "congratulations":

Bird god, king of birds,
I don't forget your birthday.
Eat enough to eat enough,
Help me for my farmer.

The "congratulations" plainly expresses the special feelings of the Yaoshan people on the birds. After the sacrifice was completed, the Yaomin family sat in the firehouse near the hall and enjoyed the rice glutinous rice with joy. After breakfast, men, women and children flocked in groups and happily went to catch the "Bird Club." The wind and rain are unstoppable and very lively.
