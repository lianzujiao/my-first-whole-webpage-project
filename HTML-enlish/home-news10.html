Sanya held a symposium on intangible cultural heritage inheritance and protection

Continuous promotion of "intangible cultural heritage" protection to shape the "cultural temperament" of the city

On the morning of July 17, a symposium on the inheritance and protection of Asian and African material cultural heritage was held in the meeting room of the municipal committee of the Chinese people's political consultative conference. Xing kongxiang, vice-chairman of the city CPPCC, attended the meeting.

Xing kongxiang said that it is urgent to strengthen the inheritance and protection of the three asian-african material cultural heritage, and governments and relevant departments at all levels of the city should attach great importance to it, further enhance their sense of responsibility and urgency, and effectively protect the three asian-african material cultural heritage.

Meeting requirements of the relevant units to fully implement the "protect, rescue first, reasonable use, inheritance development" of the guidelines, adhere to the rescue and census, the combination of protection and inheritance, the combination of the principle of combining the protection and development, adhere to the "government-led, social participation, long-term planning, implement step by step, provide and pragmatic" work method, fully tap, sorting, display "intangible" project, continue to promote the "intangible" protection, common shape cities "cultural temperament".

In order to further explore, inherit and protect the three Asian and African material cultural heritage and carry forward the excellent traditional culture, the cultural and historical committee of the CPPCC will make the inheritance and protection of the three Asian and African material cultural heritage a key research and consultation topic this year. At present, sanya has successfully applied for "intangible cultural heritage" projects, such as "li people's chaiwu" "folk songs" 13.
