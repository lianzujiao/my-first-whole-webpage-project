Taste classic feeling fashion: the 2017 Beijing xidan fashion festival is about to start

2017-07-14 Source: sohu culture 　

"Beijing xidan fashion festival" has been held and promoted for 7 years. Till now, it has become a large-scale commercial activity with the main line of xidan commercial street, radiating to the whole region, and containing popular, classic, high-end and beneficial elements. It has certain commercial influence. Xidan commercial street has also become a famous brand of Beijing business and a bellwether of capital fashion.

To further enhance the reputation and fashion of the city, the commercial influence, give full play to the xidan commercial street fashion specialty and advantage of brand cluster construction of perfect wisdom business circle, in accordance with the act in an opera in "government plays, association, enterprise" principle, adhere to the "fashion, culture, quality, life" concept of the event, the Beijing xicheng district general chamber of commerce, Beijing xidan chamber of commerce as the organizer of activity, the district party committee propaganda department, district business committee, district municipal ShiRongWei, district public security bureau, west chang 'an street agency, and other government departments, under the support of Teamed up with the zone diet industry association and large comprehensive shopping malls, focus on old, scale supermarket, catering enterprises, starting from July 19 to September 18, roll out ceremoniously "classic taste, feel the fashion" as the theme of "1 + 3" series of theme activities, namely: an opening ceremony and "cool fashion, fashion seminars, fashion food" three sections theme activities. Key contents include:

1. Held the opening ceremony of "2017 Beijing xidan fashion festival" and the launching ceremony of the fashion section of the fashion industry

The opening ceremony of the 2017 Beijing xidan fashion festival will be held on the fifth floor of galeries lafayette on July 19. Release the event information of the "2017 Beijing xidan fashion festival", invite the participating leaders and guests to jointly launch the "2017 Beijing xidan fashion festival", and at the same time open the curtain of the "tide and cool fashion" section.

The fashion section includes three parts:

First is to hold the "2017 fashion boutique show" and the launch ceremony of the fashion section.

Second, the key shopping malls in xidan area, as the sub-venue of the fashion sector, respectively carry out theme activities of different styles in different time periods.

From July 29 to August 6, juntai department store held the "spinning fashion festival", specially invited the new leisure sports brand SPACE CYCLE, combined with well-known sports brands, to jointly carry out music fitness experience, and featured the fashion sports series theme activities.

From August 15 to August 22, Joy City of xidan held the "crazy wave street culture festival" indoor street art exhibition in the street sample, which invited graffiti artists from Beijing, Hong Kong, Taiwan and France to participate in promotional activities such as the runaway bride wedding dress show to show trendy art.

September 15-16: galeries lafayette 2017 autumn/winter fashion trends released. Hold international fashion brand fashion show every day.

Third, the key retail enterprises in the region will carry out in the store for 2 months of rich content, various forms of consumer promotion activities. We will jointly construct a fashion consumption feast centered in xidan region, radiating the whole region to benefit the common people.

2. The fashion weidan seminar was held

Before the end of July, the "fashion xidan symposium" will be jointly organized by the information center of China federation of commerce, xicheng district federation of commerce and Beijing xidan chamber of commerce in xidan museum.

The symposium, around the "city commercial quality improvement and innovation and development" theme, will invite the famous experts, as the core in the capital, xicheng district, commercial development how to strengthen the competitive power of the composite, how to improve quality, innovative business model wisdom, how to play to the xidan commercial street in the leading role of regional economic development and other issues for discussion.

3. Fashion food plate

The fashion and food section mainly includes two parts:

One is to hold "xidan popular food selection and technology display and fashion food plate launch ceremony". It is planned to be held on the 8th floor of xixi friendship hotel in early August. At the launch ceremony, experts commented on the entries and announced the results of the selection, presenting the award for the popular cuisine. At the same time, the site showcases fine cuisine skills and fashionable dishes.

Second, more than 120 fashionable catering brands and catering time-honored brands in xidan will cooperate with boc, icbc, CCB and China merchants to carry out a month-long food consumption card discount campaign.

Welcome consumers to actively participate in the fashion festival activities, in the colorful summer, to fully enjoy our warm, affordable, cheerful shopping experience.
