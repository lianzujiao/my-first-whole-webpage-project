Guilin Wenchang

　


The Guangxi Literary Field is a kind of ensemble type of lyrics, referred to as the literary field, also known as the "wenwan". It is popular in the Guanyin area of ​​Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, especially in Guilin, Liuzhou, Yishan, Pingle and Zhangpu. The "text field" is named after the "martial arts" of the Qing dynasty.

The music in Guangxi is elegant and the music is fresh and beautiful. It is smooth and delicate, and the traditional music is rich. The complete set of singers include "Yu Ji", "Xi Xiang Ji", "Dream of Red Mansions", etc. The single singer has about 60 out of "Shuangxiashan" and "Wang Po Chicken". Duan Zi has nearly 100 such as "Wu Er Tang Brother" and "Gui Dian Drunk". The new works include "Jiangzhuyu", "Han Ying sees the mother", "Jiang Jie enters the mountain", and "Dragon's Passion".
