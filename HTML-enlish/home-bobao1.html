The 60th anniversary of the founding of the autonomous region of Hong Kong and Macao series of publicity and cultural activities began

Source: guangxi news network - guangxi daily　

To celebrate the 60th anniversary of the founding, the 40th anniversary of reform and opening up and the autonomous region on July 2 to 20, the autonomous region party committee propaganda department, the united front work department under the state council in conjunction with the guangxi daily, people's broadcasting station in guangxi, guangxi TV, guangxi publishing media group, performing arts groups in guangxi, guangxi and guangdong and guangxi theatre academy in Hong Kong, Macao, such as series of propaganda and cultural activities, to show Hong Kong and Macao and guangxi cadastral folks from all walks of life in jinping new era under the ideological guidance of socialism with Chinese characteristics, the new style of the new era of reform and development of guangxi.On the night of July 2 and 3, the first of a series of activities -- the zhuang fantasy and acrobatic drama "song of birds" by guangxi performing arts group was staged in Hong Kong hung hom stadium, attracting tens of thousands of Hong Kong audiences to enjoy the ethnic customs of the beautiful zhuang town, feeling the beautiful story of the development of guangxi and the deep feelings of a family in guangxi.
