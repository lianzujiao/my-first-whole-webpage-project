In August, the two international competitions were seamlessly linked and the "Shanghai culture" brand was launched

In Shanghai in August, art masters and young dreamers from around the world gathered here. The sixth Shanghai international ballet competition just concluded on the evening of August 11, and the second isaacs tern international violin competition was officially opened on the same day. Shencheng welcome sent, wonderful non-stop.

Founded in 1995, Shanghai international ballet competition is the earliest international ballet competition in China. Although it has experienced a stoppage in the middle of the competition, since 2016, it has become a world-class ballet competition in Asia, with two consecutive sessions and increasing influence. The goal is to become a world-class ballet competition in Asia through three consecutive international, professional and popular competitions with higher level. The two-year-old Shanghai Isaac stern international violin competition has risen to prominence, and the century-old American classical music magazine Musical America Worldwide has listed it as "the world's top music event guide of 2018". In the future, it aims to establish the Shanghai standard of international music competition and enhance the world power of China music.
