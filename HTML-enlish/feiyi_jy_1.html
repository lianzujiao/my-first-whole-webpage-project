brocade

　


Zhuang brocade is a famous folk craft fabric of zhuang nationality. As early as the song dynasty, it was one of the four famous brocade in China, and it was called "guangxi brocade" at that time.Zhuang brocade has a long history and a long history.According to the biography of nanman in the later han dynasty, as early as the han dynasty, the ancestors of the zhuang nationality have woven "bamboos", which is the forerunner of "zhuang brocade".Zhuang brocade is the traditional folk handicraft treasure, is one of the ancient outstanding cultural heritage of zhuang nationality, has important value.

In the song dynasty, zhou qufei wrote in the book "answer to the outside world" : "yongzhou is very beautiful, with white mastication, square white veins, wide width and large wisps. It is just like wenluo in the capital of China.White vermillion refers to zhuang brocade.Qing qianlong "to shun zhili state records (now jingxi county, guangxi) records:" married dowry, the quilt surface can not be less, from the ability of the original, soil brocade is mainly willow velvet, with five colors, thick and durable."The native brocade is the local zhuang brocade, which was an indispensable dowry when the woman got married at that time.According to historical data and research, jingxi zhuang brocade was formed in the tang and song dynasties and prevailed in the Ming and qing dynasties.By the end of the qing dynasty and the early republic of China in the decline phase.After liberation as a result of the national policy to promote the excavation of folk national handicrafts.
