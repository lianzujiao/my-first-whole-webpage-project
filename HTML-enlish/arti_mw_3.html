灯舞

　


The lantern dance is a distinctive dance of the jing nationality, performed in the last two days of the ha festival of the ha ethnic group.Is a religious dance, performed 4 - eight girl was dressed in a white gown, black trousers, head and hands with plates, plates on ShuYou lighted candles, the tempo of the dancers with drums and dance, at the same time a wrist, crisscrossed with, candles with white photograph reflect, constitute a colorful, elegant and moving picture.

The dance is after continuing "sit dream", four "peach gu" of pink long gown, will perform "light dance" for "entertainment god".They put the candles on their heads, on their palms, and dance to the music of the drum.The dancing moves focus on the arms and wrists, the upper body is straight, the knees slightly bend, the round field step of the whole dance team is flowing like the flowing water, and the hand lamp of double rotation, like the fluttering of fireflies, the flow of light makes people dizzy.Every hand and foot of the girls reflects the reverence, piety and love of life of the people of Beijing.The sight reminded people of the ancient custom of the jing people to "sacrifice candles to the gods" and "return to the ship at night".The end of "lantern dance" opened the gate of the singing of the chorus of people inside and outside the pavilion. As the tide of singing rose and fell, people's voices and laughter from near and far were gradually added, forming a sound of peace in the world floating to the vast night sky.
