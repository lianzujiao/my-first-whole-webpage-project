The Hong Kong book fair will feature books and cultural events from various countries

Hong Kong, July 17 (xinhua) (Ding Ziyi HongXueHua) the 29th Hong Kong book fair will be held between 18 and 24 in the Hong Kong convention and exhibition center, attracted 39 countries and regions this year of about 680 exhibitors, including 33 countries and Hong Kong consulate culture promotion agency will appear "villages" international exhibition, bring fans a multinational distinctive books and cultural products.

Hong Kong trade development council held a media preview on 17th. Zhou qiliang, vice President of the bureau, said this year's book fair is the largest ever. Many countries will bring characteristic books and cultural performance activities for readers to meet with book friends, so that book fans can have a deeper understanding of multinational culture.

Qi-liang zhou, in the European Union offices in Hong Kong and macau, the French consulate general in Hong Kong and Macao, and the support of the Spanish consulate general in Hong Kong and Macao, book fair held again this year "lecture series window of the world", invited a number of authors from Europe to the people to share creative ideas, including a French writer chantal Thomas, Latvia writer Dennis about neff, etc.

Apart from the "world window lecture series", the book fair will continue the tradition of previous years, with seven other lecture series, including love literature, English reading and writing, children's and youth reading, etc., which invite writers from all over the world to share their writing experience with readers and conduct close communication and interaction.

The theme of this year's book fair is love literature. The gallery has set up a special exhibition named "love between literature" to introduce ten representative love literature writers of different ages in Hong Kong, including zhang ailing, yishu, Lin yanni, etc. The exhibition will feature the writers' treasures, such as out-of-print novels, manuscripts, souvenirs and excerpts from films and stage plays adapted from their works.

The other three sections of the gallery are "silk road light and shadow", "Chinese culture walk - zhejiang walk" and "cultural treasures - cantonese opera", which will bring wonderful photos of the areas along the silk road, and give an in-depth introduction to the cultural features and intangible cultural heritage of guangdong opera, the birthplace of wuyue culture.

"About 310 cultural events will be held during the book fair, covering the fields of history, literature, art and culture of various countries. It is hoped that readers can improve their cultural attainment, broaden their horizons and understand the cultures of various countries through reading." "Said zhou qiliang.
