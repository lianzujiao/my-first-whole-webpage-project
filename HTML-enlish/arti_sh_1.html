Ma tengjiao (a famous contemporary painter in guangxi)

Ma tengjiao (1952 --), no. Cangchi, master of mojuntang, master of qingyun pavilion, native of changzhou, jiangsu province, born in Beijing, a famous painter and scholar in contemporary guangxi.Ma wan chang sun, son of ma huixian and lu mushi, is a famous modern calligrapher of ma tengjiao system.Currently, he is the President of ma wan's Chinese painting art research association and President of yihai huilan society.Since childhood school painting, painting style inherited the characteristics of ma's school of painting beautiful and elegant.His main works include "shuangshou tu", "canhai morning reading", "fu shang jia fu", "li bai's drunkenness", etc.His works have been widely spread in Britain, Australia, Canada, the United States, Brazil, Argentina, Germany, South Africa, Malaysia, Japan, Singapore, Hong Kong, Macao and Taiwan, and won many praises
