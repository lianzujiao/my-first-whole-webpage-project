Combining measures with measures according to local conditions and forming a joint force "implementation opinions on the entrance of opera on campus"

Recently, the publicity department of the CPC central committee, the ministry of education, the ministry of finance and the ministry of culture jointly issued the "opinions on the implementation of opera on campus" (hereinafter referred to as "opinions").

"Opinion" is the guiding ideology and basic principle of the eighteen big and fully implement the party's sixth plenary session of 3, 4, 5, 18 spirit, carry out general secretary xi series of important speech spirit, the full implementation of the party's education policy, the basic task for khalid ents, stick to the position of Chinese culture, inheriting Chinese culture gene, strengthen opera general universal education, enhance students understanding of the traditional opera art and experience, and guide students to establish correct aesthetic idea, cultivate noble moral sentiment and cultivate strong national emotion, promote the all-round development of students, build good environment for the development of the opera tradition. Adhere to advanced culture, strict guidance; Insist on educating people for all; Adhere to local conditions, multi-measures; Adhere to coordination and form synergy.

"Opinions" requirement, the national primary and secondary schools should be combined with school education and the actual different age paragraph the students physical and mental characteristics, choose good, classic, suitable for students to watch the drama art, insist on more measures simultaneously, in principle to organizational performances and opera performing arts groups into campus students entered the theater watch is given priority to, at the same time, combining with all kinds of public performances, opera into rural culture and traditional opera culture huimin activities such as lectures, and via the Internet and multimedia to watch classic, a variety of forms such as drama animation. The drama performance should be combined with the form of explanation and demonstration to strengthen the popularization of education.

Local publicity, education, and cultural departments should carry out a wide variety of forms of the school drama education activities. All kinds of schools at all levels should be close to the campus life, enrich the art education classroom, according to the students' cognitive level and psychological characteristics, actively explore innovation era characteristics, forms of drama education campus characteristics and the characteristics of students, such as drama, opera performance show, opera masters into campus appreciation (lectures), and other group activities, the formation of local characteristics and the traditional of our school. All levels of drama art performing groups, culture (art) pavilions, etc., shall organize students' drama activities, carry out drama rehearsal and observation, role and career experience interaction, etc., to stimulate students' interest in drama art.

"Opinion" regulation, the central propaganda department, the ministry of education, ministry of finance, the ministry of culture around the guidance and support to carry out the drama into the campus, the provinces (autonomous regions and municipalities) to establish led by publicity department, education, finance, culture and other departments involved in the drama into the campus work organization and coordination mechanism, individual, participate in department coordination promote the work of the specific implementation. Among them, local publicity departments, together with education, culture and other departments shall publish the catalogue of drama (festival) on campus every three years through the website before November 30 of last year.

The qualification of performing arts groups and related social groups shall be examined and approved by local cultural departments every three years according to relevant measures. The department of culture issued the review notice through the website before December 10 of last year. Any group that meets the requirements for application shall sign up for the unified evaluation of the qualification of entering the campus of the opera according to the requirements of the announcement. The cultural department has invited art and education experts to review the group qualification, determine the list of the groups that have obtained the qualification of opera on campus, and publish the list to the public through the website before December 30 of last year. The publicity departments at all levels shall, together with education, finance and culture departments, formulate the on-campus program of the drama in that year and organize the implementation according to the published catalogue of drama (festival) and the list of performing group qualification. The cultural department should guide all kinds of opera performing groups and relevant social resources at all levels to implement the tasks undertaken according to education requirements, ensure quality, and listen to the opinions and Suggestions of the majority of schools and students to constantly improve the level.

"The opinions" pointed out that the government, schools, society and individuals should be fully mobilized to pool resources and jointly support the opera entering the campus. We should firmly establish the guiding ideology of "safety first", strengthen safety education and management, and establish and improve the safety management system. Education departments at all levels shall include opera on campus in the annual work content of education, and take students' participation in drama practice as an important part of school aesthetic education practice. (journalist liu miao)
