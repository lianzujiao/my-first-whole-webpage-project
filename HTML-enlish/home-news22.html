And the race against time: rescue record with inheritance as the core

　"There is nothing we can do about the loss of time and time, but all we can do is race against time to save our precious cultural heritage." "Zhang qingshan said.

"When the rescue recording work was initiated, qin dexiang was diagnosed with cancer. Nine months after the project started, the old man died. Fortunately, we finally completed all the collection work before his death." This is what pan zhen, director of the rescue recording project of qin dexiang, a representative inheritor of changzhou recitation, wrote in his shooting script.

According to the national library of China memory project director, what inspection team leader Tian Miao introduction, starting in 2015, national library of China memory project center as a academic consultation and acceptance of the retrieved records work organization, has written the code for the inheritance and people work records retrieved from national representative and the inheritance and people work records retrieved from national representative operational guidelines, held the project implementation team training, provides the project team and implement standard operation reference.

In September 2017, the China memory project center organized 23 experts on intangible cultural heritage, 6 documentary experts and 5 literature experts to form an acceptance expert committee, which began to conduct acceptance inspection on the first batch of 238 rescue records in 2015. As of May 2018, there are 202 qualified projects and 25 outstanding projects.

Salvage record of intangible cultural heritage is a systematic project involving multiple disciplines and fields. The academic expert team plays a crucial role in every link from shooting to acceptance. Liaoning province was named the excellent achievements of wang xiuxia - medicine lushan manchu paper-cutting project, its academic commissioner is the liaoning province civil culture and artists association former vice President wang guang. According to the report, during the filming process, wang guang vacated his daughter's studio for the shooting team to use for free. "With such an excellent and dedicated scholar as the project academic specialist, the academic level of the project achievements can be imagined high." "Tian miao told reporters.

"Two achievements have been made in the rescue recording of intangible cultural heritage. The second is to explore a set of practice models of intangible cultural heritage construction involving the state, expert teams and social forces.
