"Finding Dreams, Liu Sanjie" premiered in Yizhou, Guangxi, naked eye 3D into reality

People's Network Yizhou 8-21 Electric (Wang Yong) 　

On the evening of August 20th, Guangxi's first large-scale naked-eye 3D holographic projection song and dance show party - "Looking for Dreams, Liu Sanjie" will premiere at the Yizhou Gymnasium.

Liu Sanjie is the summer theme of the 2016 Yizhou Liu Sanjie Cultural Tourism Festival. It aims to vividly reproduce the songs of Liu Sanjie by integrating the new technological means with the theme of “Guihai Yizhou·Singing Liu Sanjie”. Origin, carry forward, carry forward and inherit experience, and build Yizhou Liu Sanjie cultural tourism brand. The whole performance is based on the "Seeking Dreams and Three Sisters" as the main line. Through the ingenious conversion of 3D video and holographic projections, different story scenes are created, and the real and fake people are transformed into technology, so that Liu Sanjie and the modern Liu Sanjie in the movie meet each other. Fantasy is now in Yizhou. In the specific structure, the whole party was composed of "Preface, Three Sisters, Dreams, Echoes", "Teng Tangle Tree, Love Silk Road", "New Three Sisters, a City That Can Sing", "Ending, Dream Back to Yizhou" "The four chapters are composed.

"Looking for Dreams, Liu Sanjie" is the theme of the summer activities of the Liu Sanjie Cultural Tourism Festival. It is based on the theme of "Guihai Yizhou·Singing Liu Sanjie" and the "Three Dreams of Sisters" as the main line, through the use of advanced 3D video. With holographic projection technology, we can create different story scenes and realize the on-the-spot conversion of true and false people. Let the young girl Liu Sanjie in the film 56 years ago realize the time and space crossing, and perform live singing on the stage with the representative strings of the contemporary Chinese style. Reproduce the origin, development, promotion and inheritance of Liu Sanjie's song culture. Through the combination of technology and art, the whole performance will be able to graft and integrate Yizhou Liu Sanjie's song culture and tourism resources, and present Liu Sanjie's song culture in a fresh and highly artistic form.
