Interpretation of guangxi cultural administration law enforcement notification system (trial)

The guangxi cultural administration law enforcement notice system (trial) (hereinafter referred to as the system (trial)) has been deliberated and adopted by the party group meeting of the cultural department of the autonomous region, which will take effect from November 28, 2017. "System (trial)" will further clear specification culture told the behavior of administrative law enforcement to ensure that the cultural administrative law enforcement open, fair and justice, and safeguard cultural administration relative person's right to know, to participate and to choose, from the system (try out) "told the range of subjects, way and not be told, inform program, etc., provides general law enforcement main body make a demonstrative obligation of administrative act.

I. background of the establishment of the system (trial)

Set the culture system of administrative law enforcement told that guangxi (try out) "is autonomous region should conscientiously implement the autonomous region party committee, the government issued by the guangxi zhuang autonomous region government under the rule of law construction plan (2016-2020)" (GuiFa [2016] no. 24) deployment requirements, the implementation of the autonomous region comprehensively promote administration according to law work leading group about 2017 OuZhi the realistic need, which the authority for examination and assessment of the rule of law government construction work (guangxi administration according to law do hair [2017] no. 3), as well as strengthen the administrative law enforcement in our culture, regulate the behavior of cultural administrative law enforcement to inform, The important measures to ensure the openness, fairness and fairness of the enforcement of cultural administration are of great practical significance for the establishment and improvement of the administrative law enforcement information system of the cultural administration in our region and the protection of the right to know, participate and choose.

II. Necessity of making the system (trial)

First, the party committee of autonomous region, government rule by law government construction needs. In accordance with the deployment requirements of the implementation plan for the construction of the rule of law government of guangxi zhuang autonomous region (2016-2020) (GUI fa [2016] no. 24) issued by the party committee and government of the autonomous region, the administrative organs of the autonomous region should strengthen the construction of the administrative law enforcement procedure system and regulate the administrative law enforcement actions by the end of 2017. We will implement the sponsor system for administrative law enforcement cases, establish systems for hearing major and difficult cases, and for collective discussion, so as to improve the quality of handling cases. Establish and implement the administrative law enforcement investigation and evidence collection system, information system and the administrative law enforcement assistance system in different places. We will establish a recording system for the entire process of administrative law enforcement to ensure that all aspects of law enforcement are documented. We will establish a legal review system for major administrative law enforcement decisions, scientifically define the scope, contents and procedures of such review, and ensure that major administrative law enforcement decisions are lawful and reasonable

Second, we need to further standardize the investigation and evidence collection of cultural administration. The administrative punishment law and the administrative license law in China have established the administrative law enforcement notification system in the legal form. Cultural administrative law enforcement to inform system is an important part of administrative told system, it is accompanied by the establishment and development of administrative told system, is a culture in the process of administrative law enforcement in law enforcement, the culture of administrative law enforcement behavior related content through legal procedures to the other party on public display, in order to make the administrative relative person to know and to give the other party in accordance with the law to defend, present opportunity program behavior of a kind of administration, administrative law enforcement and culture in the process of cultural administrative law enforcement shall perform a legal obligation.

III. The feasibility of formulating the system (trial)

First, establish the system (try out) "is in the cultural practice of administrative law enforcement work, combined with work reality, according to the law of the People's Republic of China on administrative punishment law, the administrative licensing law of the People's Republic of China" and other relevant laws, regulations, rules and regulations, formulate the told the range of subjects, way and not be told, inform program, etc., the law enforcement main body to promote our culture open and transparent operation of administrative law enforcement standard culture of administrative law enforcement behavior, administrative law enforcement open, fair and justice, and ensure that culture safeguard cultural administration relative person's right to know, to participate and to choose, Give full play to the role of notification in administrative enforcement. Secondly, the formulation of the "system (trial)" is to form the first draft of the document on the basis of full demonstration, and through the portal of the department of culture of the autonomous region, extensively solicit the opinions of the municipal departments of cultural administration and the public.
