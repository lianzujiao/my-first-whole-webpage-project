Chili bone

　


In the custom of the Miao nationality, killing the pig every year, a must-do dish. The Miao people called it "Ai Knock"; the Zhuang, Han, Yi, Sui, and Yi compatriots were called "chili bones." The bone of the pepper is smashed with the bones of the pig, cow, or other beast that has been killed. Mix with dried chili powder, ginger, pepper, allspice, wine, salt, etc., and seal it in the altar. After half a month (the longer the storage time) The better the edible food, the more delicious and spicy, it can increase appetite, protect the wind from cold, prevent colds, is a standing food for Miao, and is also a good treat for hospitality.

In the custom of the Miao nationality, the pigs are killed every year. The bones of the pigs are chopped, mixed with chili powder, and the appropriate amount of salt, wine and pepper are mixed. Then put it in the jar and seal it. After one month, it will be edible.
