Mashan

　


Mashan county is located in the central part of guangxi, in the southern bank of the hongshui river, the northern foot of daming mountain.Luo yue's hometown of longevity.It borders shanglin county and xincheng county in the east, dahua county and pingguo county in the west, wuming county in the south and duan yao autonomous county in the north.The county now has jurisdiction over 4 townships in 7 towns, 145 villages and 6 communities, with a total population of 510,000, 9 ethnic groups including zhuang, han and yao, covering a total area of 2,363 square kilometers, which is a typical large stone mountain area with relatively weak economic foundation.

The county has 11 ethnic compatriots, including zhuang, han, yao and miao, with a large population of zhuang.Among them, the main nationalities of zhuang, han and yao are the people living in the world. They each have their own language, and native speakers mainly talk in their native language.However, most adults of the han and yao ethnic groups also use zhuang or Chinese southwestern mandarin as communication tools.Public meetings or major events organized by the government, often in southwest mandarin.Characters are most commonly used in Chinese.Some zhuang people are also familiar with their own language.The domestic language has zhuang, southwest mandarin, pinghua, hakka (xinmin), and so on.
