Yao's tambourine, a song hall night, appeared at the China folk art festival

　


Recently, the ninth Chinese opera festival was held in tianjin.Selected by the experts organized by the China qu association, recommended by the guangxi qu association and selected by the hezhou wenlian and pinggui wenguang sports bureau, the song tang night, a song and drum show of yao nationality, was selected as the special show for the ninth China qu and art festival.This is the third time that the program has been selected to appear on the national folk art stage after being selected into the second China western excellent folk art show and won the second prize of the 15th China population culture award.

The 9th China opera festival is jointly sponsored by the Chinese federation of literature and arts, the Chinese writers and artists association, the publicity department of the CPC tianjin municipal committee of the CPC, and the tianjin federation of literature and arts.The art and music festival gathers the representative music from all over the country and its excellent performance.As the opening show, "song tang night" was presented as the second performance of the national excellent folk art program. With the strong ethnic customs and distinctive local characteristics of bayi zhuang town, it demonstrated the theme of The Times of the inheritance and innovation of national culture, and brought the audience of tianjin, an important city of folk art, with fresh artistic aesthetic feelings, which attracted the attention of the industry.Relevant personnel of the program also received an exclusive interview with tianjin TV international channel. Through the national professional stage and internationally oriented TV programs, the unique national culture was widely disseminated at home and abroad.(source: nanning news network -- nanning evening news reporter li zongwen)
