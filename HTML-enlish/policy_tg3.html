[广西]Haisi Culture Promotes Ocean Tourism 2018 Maritime Silk Road (Beihai)

2018-08-02 22:11 source:Guangxi News Network 　


Li Bin, He Guangkai, Wang Naixue, Cai Jinjun, Gan Lin, Xu Xing, etc. attended the meeting; on May 14th, jointly sponsored by the Asia-Pacific Tourism Association, the Autonomous Region Tourism Development Committee, the Beihai Municipal People's Government, and the China Tourism News, “The Road to the Sea, Haisi Culture and the Ocean The tourism-themed 2018 Maritime Silk Road (Beihai) Tourism Industry Development Investment Conference was held in Beihai. Li Bin, vice chairman of the autonomous region, He Guangkai, president of the Asia-Pacific Tourism Association, Wang Naixue, secretary of the Beihai Municipal Party Committee, director of the Standing Committee of the Municipal People's Congress, Cai Jinjun, mayor, and Xu Xing, president of China Tourism News, attended the meeting. Gan Lin, Director of the Tourism Development Committee of the Autonomous Region, presided over the opening meeting.

In his speech, Li Bin said that in recent years, Guangxi has seized the opportunity and actively integrated into the construction and cooperation of the “Belt and Road”. The tourism economy and cultural exchanges and cooperation have deepened and deepened, and it has become one of the regions with the most active domestic and international tourism and investment cooperation. Beihai is an important starting port for the ancient Maritime Silk Road in China. When General Secretary Xi Jinping inspected Beihai last year, he proposed to vigorously develop the "seaward economy" and strive to write a new chapter in the 21st century Maritime Silk Road. He hopes that this conference will add a new touch of color to the new chapter of the Maritime Silk Road in Guangxi and Beihai, and become a grand gathering to deepen and carry forward the "One Belt and One Road" under the profound sea silk culture of the North Sea and Guangxi, and become the leader. The Belt and Road Initiative has become a grand event to promote the integration of culture and industry.

Wang Naixue expressed warm welcome to the experts and guests who came to the meeting. He said that the North Sea is one of the originating ports of the ancient Chinese Maritime Silk Road and is an important node city connecting the ASEAN countries in the National “Belt and Road” initiative. The Beihai Municipal Party Committee and the Municipal Government have always attached great importance to the development of tourism. Especially since the first batch of countries were selected to create a global tourism demonstration city, we have taken the “implementation of a global tourism city construction conference war” as a starting point, vigorously promote tourism projects and comprehensively improve tourism. The environment, focus on improving service quality, the city's tourism development into the "fast lane." At present, Beihai is entering a critical period of accelerated development, and the development of tourism industry in the North Sea has also ushered in a rare golden opportunity period. Beihai will take this meeting as an important opportunity to further seize opportunities, intensify efforts, and strive to create a bright future for tourism development in the North Sea.
