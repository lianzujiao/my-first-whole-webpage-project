Zhuang brocade technique is one of the traditional handicraft techniques in China.The technique of zhuang brocade was formed in the tang and song dynasties, and further developed in the Ming and qing dynasties.After thousands of years of development, the zhuang brocade has its own system of three categories, more than 20 varieties and more than 50 patterns, with sturdy durability, exquisite craftsmanship, unique patterns, exquisite patterns.

On May 20, 2006, zhuang brocade technique was approved by the state council to be included in the first batch of national intangible cultural heritage list.

zhuang brocade pattern is exquisite, colour is flowery, it is exquisite handicraft, have very high practical value again.Zhuang brocade is famous at home and abroad for its unique patterns, brilliant colors, strong and durable, and strong national characteristics.The variety of zhuang folk brocade includes bedding face, bed blanket, harness, hanging bag, tablecloth, apron, headscarf, clothing accessories, etc.

The weaving technology,

The zhuang people have worked out a whole set of brocade weaving techniques in the long term.They use the hand loom equipped with support system, transmission device, splitter device and jacquard device, which is made of cotton yarn and various colored velour as weft. The traditional small wooden machine, also known as zhuang nationality bamboo cage machine, is equipped with a "flower cage" to pick up the pattern patterns, and the largest feature of zhuang brocade loom is the flower cage.

Zhuang jin colour

"Red with green, see not common", this reflected zhuang brocade artisan to the zhuang brocade match some requirements: should be gorgeous and dazzing, but also durable to see.Zhuang people are fond of many colors. They take red, yellow, blue and green as their basic colors and the rest as complementary colors.Use green as foil, have cheerful sentiment;If with yellow green configuration, gorgeous and moving.A zhuang brocade, often with a few colors or even a dozen of color collocation composition, because configuration is proper, appear gorgeous, rich and unified.Contrast harmony, ancient and deep, Chinese and not common.

Zhuang jin pattern

The pattern that zhuang brocade designs composition has 3 kinds roughly: it is the geometrical grain that weave two square continuous and square continuous on plain grain, composition is continuous geometrical design, appear simple and lively;Second, it is based on a variety of geometric patterns, decorated with animal and plant patterns, to form a multi-level composite pattern, clear patterns and a sense of relief;The third is to use a variety of geometric size combination, square and circle interwoven into a dense and rhythmic complex geometric patterns, with the beauty of rigorous harmony;
