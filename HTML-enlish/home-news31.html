Shengshi huacheng dance guangxi

Shengshi huacheng, dancing guangxi. On July 9, in order to promote excellent dance works and talents to continuously emerge and promote the prosperity and development of dance art, celebrating the 60th anniversary of the founding of guangxi zhuang autonomous region, the fourth guangxi young dancer competition in five days started in liuzhou city.

Founded in 2010, guangxi young dancers competition is a grand event in the field of dance art in the whole region. It builds a platform for dancers to show their talents and realize their dreams. Through the biennial competition, it is found that a large number of creative and performing talents have been cultivated. This competition has played an important role in improving the level of dance creation and performance in guangxi and promoting the prosperity and development of dance art. The event aims to thoroughly implement xi jinping thought on socialism with Chinese characteristics for a new era and the 19th party spirit, and to show the beautiful aspirations of people of all ethnic groups in guangxi to welcome daqing and open up the future.

This competition has strong actors and elite. 121 young dancers from 20 participating units and 76 dance works entered the final. Competitors have a song and dance theatre, nanning in guangxi art theatre art theatre, theatrical performances in guilin, liuzhou city co., LTD., China, performing arts co., LTD., such as the autonomous region, municipal art performers including many renowned professional in vitro, and protection of heritage and bobai county folk culture art center, tianlin intangible heritage protection performance center, jingxi county zhuang intangible protection such as the unit at the county level, nationality become more widely. The dancers, with the spirit of ten years to sharpen their skills, prepared actively and practiced assiduously, fully displayed a solid artistic foundation in this competition. The dance works are rooted in the fertile soil of life, filled with a strong sense of life, reflecting a good unity of thought, art and appreciation. All the matches benefit the public with low ticket price, and bring the spirit of beauty to the people.
