Zhuang nationality culture

　


The Xingbin District (original Binxian County) has a long history, ancient forms, diverse forms, and rich connotations. The temples of Buddhism (that is, Hua Po, Hualin, Po, Wang Niangniang) are the main carriers. Worship constitutes the Zhuang nationality culture system. The Buddhism belief originated from the flower totem worship of the primitive society. The Buddhism worship in Xingbin District was formed at the end of the Yuan Dynasty and the Ming Dynasty, and reached its peak in the late Ming Dynasty to the Qing Dynasty and the Republic of China.

The Zhuang ethnic culture has a high historical and cultural value, academic research value and development and utilization value. It is very meaningful to study the origin and development of the Zhuang nationality and the origin and development of the civilization of the Pearl River basin, adapt to people's beliefs, seek the roots of the ancestors and pilgrimages, and enrich the cultural life of the people. Protect the culture of Zhuang nationality, liberate traditional fertility ideas, establish a civilized marriage and new style, respect women, protect the legitimate rights and interests of women and children, enhance women's overall quality, improve women's social status, political status and economic status, and build harmony Social aspects also have important practical significance.
