Yanxia mountain scenic spot is located in yandun town, lingshan county, qinzhou city, guangxi, 100 kilometers away from nanning, 80 kilometers away from qinzhou, 35 kilometers away from lingshan county, guangxi.

Yanxia mountain scenic area is rich in natural ecological flora and fauna resources, such as wild boar, pig-zihu, egret, leopard cat and other rare wild animals, as well as a variety of rare Chinese herbal medicine plants such as wild dendrobium, honeysuckle, tianqi, and lingzhi.The scenic spot adheres to the construction concept of combining the countryside and ecology, integrates the local folk culture characteristics, carries out the exhibition of intangible cultural projects such as jumping ridge head and yandun drum performance, and sets up folk custom museum and various popular science education activity venues.Equipped with outdoor barbecue, farmhouse joy, litchi and other crop picking, camping, sports development, animal fun and other wild projects.It is a good choice for parent-child play, outdoor development and rural ecological play.
