"New era civilization teaching center" was unveiled in hengxian county, guangxi

Zhongxin.com, guangxi news, July 31 (yu daofeng) hengxian county, nanning, guangxi, recently held the unveiling ceremony of the "new era civilization education center" in the party school of the county party committee, which marked the official establishment of hengxian "new era civilization education center".

Huang haitao, party secretary of hengxian county, zeng pengxin, director of the standing committee of the county people's congress jiang xiaoqi, chairman of the county CPPCC xue wen and other county leaders attended the ceremony.

Kun Huang Haitao pointed out that the establishment "the new era of civilization study center", is to strengthen heng important platform for the construction of socialist spiritual civilization, improve the masses culture and moral quality, promote social management modernization important channel, its function orientation is in pays special attention to the self building, organize various older forms of civilization at the same time, strengthen to the township (town), village (community) and agatanoatae units to create a "new era of assembly" guidance, coordination, training and service.

According to huang, the main tasks of the "new era civilization education center" are to highlight the ideological construction and value guidance, to enter the mind with new ideas, to enter the household into the people with the situation policy, to lead the advanced model demonstration, to carry forward the civilized social customs, and to provide voluntary services. Each member unit of the center should coordinate closely, actively participate, integrate resources from all aspects, mobilize forces from all aspects, give play to advantages of all aspects, promote the normalization and institutionalization of the work, and truly implement the requirements of the tasks of the cultural education in the new era.

At the ceremony, huang haitao presented books to hengxian county's "new era cultural heritage center".

Afterwards, the first training activity of hengxian "new era civilization training center" and the warning education class of party conduct and clean government was held. All the staff watched the typical cases of violation of discipline and law by party cadres.

Huang haitao asks in the education class, one should study deeply to understand; Second, we should insist on the use of the important experience of exercising strict party discipline in an all-round way, reorganize the party and start again, and promote the continuous development of the party discipline in an all-round way. Third, we need to fully fulfill the responsibilities of party management and party governance, abide by the responsibilities of being loyal and clean, and strive to create a sound political ecological environment featuring clean air and integrity.
