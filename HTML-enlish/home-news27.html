Yongtai has created a brand of local cultural activities to let the masses sing


Villagers to yongtai yingkou township cultural center in the rural library reading newspapers

Recently, at 7 PM every Friday night, Mr. Zhang, who lives in the town of yongtai city, would go to the local square of six pavilions and one center to participate in the "happy yongtai people sing" activity -- with beautiful melody, the audience sang song after song, which is like a small concert. Anyone who wants to sing on stage can be the leading role in the center of the stage. On Saturday nights, Mr. Zhang can also watch his favorite minju opera. "" since the 'six pavilions and one center' was built, our cultural life has been increasingly enriched." " "Said uncle zhang.

A person in charge of yongtai county's science, technology and culture bureau said they have built a number of brands of local cultural activities, so that the culture can be implemented to benefit the people, and people's cultural sense and happiness are constantly improved.

Late last year, yongtai "six pavilions one center" after the use, yongtai county science and technology culture bureau to make full use of the existing resources, the show. "" we hope to meet the requirements of different masses as much as possible, so that the 'six pavilions and one center' can be lively and truly become the center of cultural activities throughout the county." " Relevant person in charge said.

The reporter learned that the "happy yongtai everyone sing" activity launched in March this year has become a regular activity of local people every Friday night, attracting more than 500 people from 26 villages (communities) in places like zhangcheng town and chengfeng town. Since June, another weekend activity of minju in the square has been launched. Every Saturday night, the LED screen of the square will play minju to meet the needs of minju fans.

Since the campaign began, more than 200 people have been arriving every week. The event has also attracted many people from surrounding areas and distant areas.

"" museums, cultural centers, libraries and other cultural activities are also continuously carrying out colorful cultural activities. The "six pavilions and one center" with complete facilities and powerful functions has become a new cultural landmark in yongtai county. In addition to focusing on the "six pavilions and one center", yongtai county science and technology culture bureau also vigorously promoted the construction of cultural brand activities, such as "zhouzhou music mass culture activities", "mountain song duet", "culture benefiting people · liujin", "book incense yongtai national book month" and "bird's nest book house".

It is understood that "bird's nest book house" this kind of "flow reading" form of activity is fuzhou public culture service system demonstration area to create one of the characteristic highlights. Today, yongtai has set up 45 "bird's nest bookhouses" in primary and secondary schools across the county, which will be gradually promoted in public cultural places across the county.
