Miao Gulongpo Club

　


“Gulongpo Club” is a traditional festival of Miao nationality in the Sirong area of ​​Rongshui Miao Autonomous County, Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region. It has a history of more than 100 years. It is held on the 16th of each month and the 16th of August each year: the former wishes a bumper harvest and the latter celebrates a bumper harvest.

The Gu Longpo will include reed races, stepping songs, fighting horses, fighting birds, horse racing, lion dances, and singing folk songs. Among them, the reed race and the fighting horse are the most enthusiastic.

At noon, the reeds of each village circled into a circle, blowing up the cheerful and melodious reeds. The girl and the young man danced around the reeds and performed “stepping on the temple”. Sometimes thousands of people participated in singing and dancing. When fighting horses, one person first took a mare to the middle of the racecourse, and then brought two strong stallions from both sides. When the horse was about to pass by the mare, the two stallions each hugged their heads and raised their front hooves. Hey, rushing to the other side and fighting, or biting or kicking, sometimes erect both, sometimes chasing quickly, the scene is very intense and intense, and some have to fight dozens of rounds before winning, the loser withdraws, the winner again Fighting another new stallion. Finally, people rushed to congratulate the winning horsemen.

At the end of the fighting horse, the reed race entered a climax, and the villages used all the skills to show the playing skills. The judges who listened to the slopes judged the winners. Last participation The reed team of the slope meeting will be together, and the mountain will be heard, and it will ring through the sky. 　　

The Gulongpo Club is both a social event and a traditional national sports event. At that time, in addition to the Miao nationality, the nearby Zhuang, Yi, Yao, Han and other ethnic groups also came to participate.
