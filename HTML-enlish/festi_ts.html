March Three Song Festival

　


"March 3" is the biggest singer day in the Zhuang area, also known as the "Greek Festival". According to legend, it is a folk commemorative festival formed to commemorate Liu Sanjie. In 1984, the People's Government of Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region officially designated this day as a national festival for the Zhuang people - the "March 3" Song Festival. On this day of the year, a grand festival is held in Nanning, the capital of the autonomous region, and other places. During the festival, in addition to the traditional singing activities, there are also a variety of cultural and sports activities such as robbing fireworks, throwing hydrangea, touching eggs and performing dramas, dancing dragons, playing cymbals, showing movies, performing martial arts and acrobatics. . In addition, various commercial trade, investment negotiations and other activities have gradually increased, forming a new trend of "culture set up, economic singing". At that time, Lingnan Zhuang Township will gather in the four seas, and the song will be like the sea. The singer of the song, which is endless, evokes people’s thoughts and enthusiasm for the harvest, love, and happiness.

It is rumored that Liu Sanjie was a farmer of the Zhuang nationality in the Tang Dynasty. She was young and intelligent and was regarded as a "goddess." At the age of twelve, I can pass the biography, and I will sing songs and open my mouth. Self-singing and singing, the song is like a spring, beautiful and moving, without losing the temperament, so it has the reputation of "song". The folks think that they both go to immortality.
