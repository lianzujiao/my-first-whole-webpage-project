Jointly issued by five departments including the state administration of cultural heritage

　China new Internet on July 4, according to the state administration of cultural heritage website, recently, the state administration of cultural heritage, the supreme people's court, the supreme people's procuratorate, the ministry of public security, the general administration of customs jointly issued "the administrative measures for the appraisal of cultural relics involved".

The measures introduced, designed to implement the "state council on further strengthening the work of cultural relics of the guidelines" (guo fa [2016] no. 17) and the general office of the state council on further strengthening the security work of cultural relics of the implementation opinions "(countries do hair [2017] no. 81), in order to standardize the procedures of the cultural relics appraisal involved, ensure the quality of the cultural relics appraisal involved as the guidance, to better adapt to the people's court, the people's procuratorates and the public security organs and other case-handling organ for cultural relics criminal criminal cases, give full play to the cultural relics appraisal involved to support in accordance with the law to crack down on illegal and criminal activities of cultural relics.

Earlier, state administration of cultural heritage according to take effect on January 1, 2016 the supreme people's court, the supreme people's procuratorate on the breach of cultural relics management such as criminal cases to explain some issues of applicable law ([2015] no. 23), the relevant provisions of the nationwide selection specifies the 41 appraisal institutions involved in cultural relics. To further standardize the cultural relics appraisal institutions involved in identification of assessment activities, the state administration of cultural heritage, the supreme people's court, the supreme people's procuratorate, the ministry of public security and the general administration of customs in criminal law, criminal procedure law and related judicial interpretation provisions to maintain cohesion, on the basis of in view of the special circumstances of the cultural relics appraisal activities involved to jointly study the measures.

　Article is divided into six chapters 56 of the regulation, the relevant definitions and basic principles of the cultural relics appraisal involved, the scope of the cultural relics appraisal involved in the content, the institutions of the cultural relics appraisal involved in the conditions and standards, the entrusted to accept the organization and implementation of the cultural relics appraisal involved in the process, supervision and management and related implementation of the cultural relics appraisal involved in the six aspects, such as the comprehensive system of rules, with the cultural relics appraisal report format involved reference text, text and the cultural relics appraisal involved a power of attorney to build the foundation of the cultural relics appraisal involved in the management system.

　According to statistics, 41 cultural relics involved in the case have been identified in more than 1,300 criminal cases nationwide since 2016, among which more than 70,000 sets of removable cultural relics have been identified and assessed, and nearly 600 non-removable cultural relics have been identified and assessed, providing strong support for maintaining the safety of cultural relics and cracking down on crimes of cultural relics.
