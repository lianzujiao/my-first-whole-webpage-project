The district cultural work promotion symposium and cultural poverty alleviation work site meeting was held

Guangxi culture 7-31 　


From July 30th to 31st, the district cultural work promotion symposium and cultural poverty alleviation work site meeting was held in Zhangzhou City. The meeting thoroughly implemented the spirit of the Party's 19th and 19th Central and 3rd Plenary Sessions, adhered to the guidance of Xi Jinping's new era of socialism with Chinese characteristics, and implemented the major decision-making arrangements of the 19th National Congress on cultural construction and the party committee of the autonomous region. The spirit of the Fourth Plenary Session of the Eleventh Session, to convey the 2018 National Cultural and Tourism Work Symposium, the National Cultural Relics Directors' Symposium, the mid-year work conference and the spirit of the county economic development and rural revitalization promotion conference, review and summarize the work in the first half of the year, and arrange Deploy the work in the second half of the year and go all out to complete the annual target tasks. Zhang Hong, secretary of the party group and director of the Cultural Affairs Department of the autonomous region, made a speech at the meeting. Quanzhou Shou, secretary of the Cangzhou Municipal Party Committee, attended the meeting and delivered a speech.

The meeting thoroughly studied the important expositions of General Secretary Xi Jinping on culture and poverty alleviation work, especially the important content of General Secretary Xi Jinping's "four cultures" such as cultural self-confidence, cultural power, cultural road and cultural mission. . The conference conveyed the spirit of the decision-making and documentary spirit of the Ministry of Culture and Tourism on cultural poverty alleviation, emphasizing that the cultural sector must implement the decision-making arrangements of the central and autonomous regional party committees and governments on poverty alleviation, give full play to the advantages of the industry, and strengthen the poverty-stricken areas. Art creation and production, modern public cultural service system construction, cultural heritage protection and utilization, cultural industry development, cultural market supervision services, cultural exchanges and exchanges, talent team construction, implementation of the department's designated poverty alleviation work, ensuring the successful completion of the task of attacking and winning this poverty alleviation Fighting hard.
