Licheng, guangxi: 300 workers learn tai chi
Carry forward excellent traditional culture

2018-6-21 Source: guangxi news network

Liucheng county, liuzhou city, guangxi, held the promotion and training activities of staff tai chi fitness, attracting more than 300 cadres and the masses to participate actively, the scene is quite spectacular. On the same day, under the guidance of famous taijiquan teachers in chenjiagou, henan province, local cadres and the masses learned the basic posture of chen-style taijiquan together and felt the charm of tai chi culture together.
