Regulations on the protection of ethnic folk traditional culture in guangxi zhuang autonomous region

(the regulations on the protection of ethnic folk traditional culture in the guangxi zhuang autonomous region has been adopted by the 13th session of the standing committee of the 10th people's congress of the guangxi zhuang autonomous region on April 1, 2005 and is hereby promulgated, and shall come into force as of January 1, 2006.)

Chapter I general provisions

Article 1 these regulations are formulated in accordance with the relevant laws and regulations and in the light of the actual conditions of the autonomous region for the purpose of protecting, inheriting and carrying forward the fine traditional culture of the ethnic people, promoting economic and social development and promoting the building of socialist material and spiritual civilization.

Article 2 within the administrative region of the autonomous region, the following traditional ethnic and folk cultures of historical, literary, artistic, scientific and social value shall be protected by these regulations:

(1) endangered ancient Chinese characters and languages;

(2) documentation of traditional ethnic and folk cultures;

(3) typical folk oral and non-material culture such as literature, drama, folk art, music and dance;
