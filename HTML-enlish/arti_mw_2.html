Jinxiang dance

　


Jinxiang dance is a special dance of the jing nationality. It is performed in the "ha festival", an important festival of the jing nationality.The festival usually lasts for three to five days. On the first day, the most solemn "meeting god" ceremony is held, and the content of singing and dancing is the most abundant.After the start of the ceremony, the chief mourner first led the people to welcome the gods from the sea, the temple, the ancestors into the position, followed by a toast to the gods and a gift.Four ladies in pink silk gown, black trousers, and violet hair bands known as "peach gu" dance to the drum.

At the end of the first day of the ceremony, the "peach gu" once again performed the "incense dance" for the gods."Jin xiang dance" is divided into two parts, namely, worshiping the gods and entertaining the gods.Three "peach gu" left hand, three left hand burning incense, right hand performance "wheel finger hand flowers" and "flip flowers" and other beautiful dancing, in the female voice of the "song of incense," god's frequent blessing.With the change of the accompanying beat, the dance changed from the solemn and reverent "dancing incense" to the bright and cheerful "dancing music", making people enjoy the festival happiness with the gods again.
