Miao culture (melt water) ecological protection zone

Author: source: release time：2016-07-26 　

The Miao culture (melt water) ecological protection zone is mainly composed of the jurisdiction of Rongshui Miao Autonomous County and radiates to the surrounding Miao nationality. It is representative of the Miao traditional culture and other intangible cultural heritages with concentrated distribution, distinctive features, forms and connotations intact in the region. The cultural ecology of the project implements regional overall protection. It was established in 2013. The protection of the Miao culture (melt water) ecological protection zone focuses on the traditional folk activities, etiquette, festivals and related historical and cultural villages and cultural relics of the Miao nationality; knowledge and practice related to nature and the universe (such as folk knowledge, traditional medicine, etc.), folk art , folk crafts (such as painting, crafts, architecture, etc.); performing arts (such as Miao Ge, Guoha, Lusheng, fruit, etc.) and other intangible cultural heritage and living environment.

At present, there are 138 traditional festivals of various ethnic groups in the Miao culture (melt water) ecological protection zone, and there are 8 categories and 21 kinds of intangible cultural heritage, covering language, literature, craftsmanship, production and living customs, beliefs, folk songs, musical instruments, dance, Opera, arts and crafts, entertainment and other aspects. There are 1 national-level intangible cultural heritage project “Miao nationality slope group”, 9 autonomous region-level intangible cultural heritage projects “Miao nationality lighting production skills” and “Miao nationality reed production skills”, and municipal intangible cultural heritage projects. 52 items, 167 county-level intangible cultural heritage projects, 4 intangible cultural heritage training bases, 1 workshop, and 1 production demonstration base.
