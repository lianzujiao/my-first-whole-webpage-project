Guangxi sports festival fangchenggang branch opening Marine culture highlights

Fangchenggang, guangxi news network August 8 (reporter zhou longfu) "passion yangfan beibu gulf, free running sports festival"! On August 8, the 10th fangchenggang sub-venue and 2018 guangxi youth windsurfing championship opened in beautiful xiwan of fangchenggang city. This sports festival fangchenggang sub-venue incorporated local Marine elements, with a full sea view track marathon, windsurfing competition, fishing and other special events.

It is understood that the event is jointly hosted by the sports bureau of the guangxi zhuang autonomous region and the people's government of fangchenggang city, and organized by the cultural committee of fangchenggang city and the sports federation of fangchenggang city. This sports festival is an important move for fangchenggang city to implement the state council's regulations on national fitness for all, implement the national fitness implementation plan (2016-2020), respond to the national call for "national fitness day", implement the national fitness and health China strategy, and develop sports activities and enhance people's physique.

Xu dagujian, secretary of the party group and director of the cultural committee of fangchenggang city, introduced that guangxi sports festival is a major sports benefit to the people, and is a national fitness grand gathering that advocates national sports and shares health. This year, fangchenggang will also hold the second China asean international marathon while holding various sports events in various forms and rich contents. At that time, 15,000 marathon runners and hundreds of domestic and foreign media will gather in fangcheng port, which will better enhance the city's influence and further strengthen the sports culture exchanges between fangcheng port and asean countries.

According to the introduction, with the theme of "passionate yangfan beibu bay, free running sports festival", the national fitness series of fangchenggang sports festival starts from August 2018 and ends in December for 120 days. The event will continue and expand the characteristics of the first nine national, popular, scientific, fashionable and international festivals and competitions, and highlight the development concept of "loving the party and benefiting the people" and "innovation, coordination, green, open and sharing".

This year's sports festival in fangchenggang city has a richer content and wider range of events, with emphasis on scientific fitness, sports culture, healthy and happy running and other elements. The project involves more than ten items such as marathon, windsurfing, ball games, martial arts, bicycle, chess and CARDS, and sea fishing. At the same time, the activity covers community residents, rural residents, ethnic minority masses, teenagers, middle-aged and old people, government officials, enterprises and public institutions, etc., meeting the diversified sports and fitness needs of different groups.

The opening ceremony of the day featured a variety of cultural and sports activities such as taijiquan eight-section brocade, da yangge, juvenile roller skating and juvenile Latin dance, which attracted thousands of citizens and tourists to actively participate. In the following three days, more than 50 windsurfers from liuzhou, beihai, baise and other municipal teams competed fiercely.
