"Guangfu yue yun" music folk art culture series activities in guangdong-hong kong-macao greater bay area

Guangzhou daily (all media reporter huang an) the day before yesterday, "guangfu yueyun - the first cantonese song network contest and ancient poetry composition contest of music quyi culture series of guangdong-hong kong-macao greater bay area" kick-off ceremony was held in guangzhou.

It is reported, guangdong province yue yun series of singing competition set cantonese pop music special and special cantonese music, 18 years old or above can participate. The competition will firstly set up sub-division areas in guangzhou, shenzhen, foshan, zhuhai, dongguan, zhongshan, yangjiang, jiangmen, maoming, zhaoqing, shaoguan and qingyuan, and it is planned to set sub-division areas in Hong Kong, Macao and guangxi autonomous region.

　From July to October, 2018, the preliminary and preliminary competitions of the sub-division will be held, and the annual finals of each sub-division will be held in November, 2018. From December, 2018 to January, 2019, the annual finals of the cantonese song network competition and the cantonese composition competition of ancient poetry will be held in guangzhou.

Among them, the cantonese song network competition will adopt the innovative network competition way, the contestant may register through the WeChat public number.

There is another highlight in this series of activities of guangfu yueyun, which is to jointly promote the music composition of ancient Chinese treasures by musicians and creators from the three places, and promote Chinese culture through new singing of ancient poems. It is also the first time that the competition of singing tang poetry and song ci in cantonese has been set up in the cultural field of Chinese music.

It is reported that the cantonese composition competition for ancient poetry will be open to the whole society to solicit works, participants will be their own composition or music composition works uploaded to the relevant WeChat public number and competition live website.
